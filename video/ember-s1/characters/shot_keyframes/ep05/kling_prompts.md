## 클립 1: Hook + Collection (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep05_s1_hook.png`
**Scene Reference**: L5_dark_alley
**Element**: Luna

```
Medium shot of a dark cramped room interior. Bare concrete walls. Single overhead bulb hanging from wire, casting harsh yellow circle on a wooden table center frame.
Camera positioned at back wall, shooting toward a closed door at background center.

0-5 seconds: Door at background center swings inward slowly.
Bright white corridor light floods in from behind the door.
A figure in a dark coat stands in the doorway, center frame. Face hidden by backlight. Silhouette only.
Figure's shoulders squared, feet planted wide. Right hand pushes door fully open.
Camera: static, shooting straight at doorway.
Lighting: harsh yellow overhead bulb on table. White backlight from corridor behind silhouette.

5-10 seconds: Cut to medium shot. Camera positioned at right wall, shooting left.
@Luna seated at the table left side of frame, body facing right. Dark coat buttoned to collar.
Across the table right side of frame: a man hunched in a chair, body curled forward, both hands flat on table.
A small glass vase with one wilting flower sits on the table between them.
@Luna's face lit from overhead. Green eyes steady and unblinking, lips flat, chin level. Face blank.
The man's hands tremble on the table surface. The man pushes crumpled bills across the table toward @Luna with both shaking hands.
@Luna does not move. @Luna's eyes fixed on the man's face. @Luna does not blink.
Camera: static medium two-shot.
Lighting: harsh yellow overhead bulb. Deep shadows under @Luna's brow and cheekbones.

10-15 seconds: @Luna leans forward 10cm toward the man. @Luna's right hand reaches across table.
@Luna's index finger taps once on the stack of bills. @Luna tilts head 15 degrees to the right.
@Luna's lips part slightly. Eyes still steady, unblinking, locked on the man's face.
The man flinches backward in his chair. The man's shoulders hunch higher. The man looks down at the table.
@Luna straightens back in the chair. @Luna's left hand rests flat on the table.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to close-up on @Luna's face from the right side.
Lighting: harsh yellow overhead. Shadow cuts diagonally across @Luna's face as she leans forward.

Sound: door creaking open, heavy footstep on concrete, chair legs scraping, bills sliding on wood, quiet sobbing, breathing.
[Luna, quiet cold female voice, face toward the man]: "Flowers bloom better after pruning."
Pause. Sobbing from the man.
[Luna, same voice quieter, head tilted right]: "Some just die."
Long silence. Paper money rustling.
```

## 클립 2: Talent Recognition + Alley (12초)

**키프레임**: `ep05_s3_talent_recognition.png`
**Scene Reference**: L5_dark_alley
**Element**: Luna

```
Close-up of @Luna standing in a doorway, body facing forward, center frame.
Left half of @Luna's face lit by pale yellow corridor light from the left. Right half of face in shadow.
Camera positioned at chest height, 1 meter from @Luna's face, shooting straight at her.

0-4 seconds: @Luna stops mid-step in the doorway. Right foot forward, left foot still behind.
@Luna's eyes look down at her own right hand. @Luna's right hand hangs at her side, fingers slightly curled.
@Luna's lips press together. @Luna's eyebrows rise 3mm. Eyes widen slightly.
@Luna swallows once. @Luna's jaw tightens. @Luna blinks twice rapidly.
@Luna looks away from her hand, looks straight ahead past the camera.
@Luna's face goes blank. Eyes steady and unblinking, lips flat.
Camera: static close-up.
Lighting: pale yellow corridor light from the left. Dark shadow on right side of face.

4-8 seconds: Cut to medium shot. Camera positioned at end of a narrow alley, shooting toward @Luna.
Dark wet brick walls on both sides. Distant red and blue neon glow reflecting on wet ground.
@Luna stumbles out of a metal door on the right wall, three steps into the alley.
@Luna's left hand slams flat against the brick wall. @Luna bends forward at the waist.
@Luna's right hand grabs her own stomach. @Luna's shoulders heave forward.
@Luna's face pale, lips pressed tight, cheeks puffed slightly, eyes squeezed shut.
Camera: static medium shot from 3 meters away.
Lighting: dim red neon from background right. Blue neon from background left. No direct light on @Luna.

8-12 seconds: @Luna retches. @Luna's body lurches forward twice.
@Luna's left hand slides down the brick wall. @Luna's knees bend halfway.
@Luna's right hand moves from stomach to the wall for support. Both palms flat on wet brick.
@Luna's head hangs down, hair falling forward covering face. @Luna's shoulders rise and fall with heavy breathing.
@Luna stays bent forward, both hands on wall, head down. Body still.
Camera: static, same position. No movement.
Lighting: dim red and blue neon reflections on wet alley walls. Rain drips from gutters above.

Sound: footstep stopping on concrete, breath catching, door slamming shut behind,
retching, heavy breathing, hands slapping wet brick, rain dripping on pavement.
No dialogue.
```

## 클립 3: Empty Room + Duchess CCTV (12초)

**키프레임**: `ep05_s5_empty_room.png`
**Scene Reference**: L2_duchess_club
**Element**: Duchess

```
0-5 seconds: Extreme close-up of a small glass vase on a bare wooden table, center frame.
The vase is empty. No flowers. One dried petal lies flat on the table next to the vase.
The room behind the vase is dark and empty. No chairs. No people. Bare concrete walls.
Single overhead bulb casts a tight circle of yellow light on the table and vase.
Everything outside the light circle is black.
Camera: static, positioned directly overhead, shooting straight down at the table surface.
Lighting: harsh yellow overhead bulb. Sharp shadow ring around the light circle.

Sound: silence. Distant muffled traffic through walls. A clock ticking slowly.

5-8 seconds: Cut to medium shot. Camera positioned behind @Duchess's right shoulder, shooting forward.
Dark club interior. @Duchess seated in a leather chair, back toward camera, right side of frame.
@Duchess holds a wine glass in her left hand at shoulder height. Ice-blue liquid in the glass.
In front of @Duchess: a CCTV monitor on a low table, center frame. Monitor shows grainy black-and-white footage.
The monitor feed shows a small figure exiting a dark alley from a metal door. The figure walks away from camera in the feed.
@Duchess's right hand rests on the chair arm. @Duchess tilts her head 10 degrees to the left, watching the monitor.
Camera: static over-the-shoulder shot.
Lighting: cold blue neon strips along the ceiling. Monitor glow on @Duchess's right cheek. No warm light.

8-12 seconds: @Duchess lifts wine glass to lips. @Duchess takes one slow sip.
@Duchess sets wine glass down on the chair arm with her left hand.
Cut to close-up of @Duchess's face from the front, center frame.
@Duchess's lips curve into a slow smile, one corner of mouth raised higher than the other.
@Duchess's eyes half-lidded, chin raised slightly. @Duchess looks at the monitor screen below her eye line.
@Duchess's right hand lifts from the chair arm. @Duchess makes a small gesture with two fingers, pointing forward.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to close-up on @Duchess's face.
Lighting: cold blue neon from above. Monitor glow casting pale white on lower half of @Duchess's face.

Sound: monitor static hum, wine glass touching lips, liquid sipping, glass set down on leather, club bass low and distant.
[Duchess, pleased commanding female voice, slow smile, face toward the monitor]: "Give her the next one."
Silence. Monitor static continues.
```
