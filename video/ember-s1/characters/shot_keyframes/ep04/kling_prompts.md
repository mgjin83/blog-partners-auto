## 클립 1: 벤치 위의 고독 — 마일로 등장 (12초)

**키프레임**: `ep04_clip1_bench_milo.png`
**Scene Reference**: L4_park_bench
**Element**: Luna + Milo

```
Wide shot, camera positioned 10 meters away at path level, shooting straight toward a park bench.
Park bench center frame under a large bare tree. Grey overcast sky fills upper half of frame.
Fallen brown leaves scattered on bench seat and ground. Flat daylight, no shadows.

0-4 seconds: @Luna sits on the left end of the bench, body facing forward, legs together, feet flat.
@Luna's arms crossed tight over chest, hands gripping opposite elbows.
@Luna's head tilted down 30 degrees, eyes fixed on the ground in front of her feet.
@Luna's brow furrowed, lips pressed together, jaw tight. @Luna does not move.
@Luna's auburn hair falls forward partially covering her cheeks.
Wind moves the bare tree branches above. Two leaves fall from the tree onto the bench beside @Luna.
Camera: static wide shot. @Luna small in frame against the empty park.

Sound: wind through bare branches, distant traffic hum, leaves scraping on pavement.

4-8 seconds: @Milo enters frame from the right, walking fast toward the bench.
@Milo in casual dark jacket and jeans, body facing left toward @Luna.
@Milo stops 1 meter from the bench, right side of frame.
@Milo's right hand reaches forward and grabs @Luna's left upper arm.
@Luna's head snaps up. @Luna's eyes widen, eyebrows rise, mouth opens slightly.
@Luna looks up at @Milo's face. @Luna's body pulls back 10cm on the bench away from @Milo.
Camera: cuts to medium two-shot from the front, both faces visible. @Luna left, @Milo right.

Sound: fast footsteps on gravel path, arm grab — fabric compression sound, @Luna's sharp inhale.
[Milo, urgent low male voice, right hand gripping @Luna's arm, leaning toward her face]: "Luna."

8-12 seconds: @Milo leans forward, face 40cm from @Luna's face, body bent at waist.
@Milo's brow furrowed deep, eyes wide and searching @Luna's face rapidly.
@Milo's left hand rises, palm open, gesturing toward @Luna.
@Milo's nostrils flare, jaw thrust forward, lips pulled back showing teeth slightly.
@Luna stares up at @Milo. @Luna's eyes narrow, chin pulls back toward her neck.
@Luna's right hand comes up and grips @Milo's wrist on her arm.
@Luna's fingers wrap tight around @Milo's wrist but she does not pull it away yet.
Camera: holds medium two-shot, slight push-in toward both faces.

Lighting: flat grey overcast from above. No directional shadow. Muted green from grass and bare tree.

Sound: @Milo's heavy exhale, @Luna's grip on wrist — fabric and skin shifting.
[Milo, harder male voice, brow furrowed, leaning closer]: "What are you doing with Duchess's people?"
Silence. Wind gust through branches.
No dialogue from @Luna.
```

## 클립 2: 거절과 충격 (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep04_clip2_rejection.png`
**Scene Reference**: L4_park_bench
**Element**: Luna + Milo

```
Medium close-up, camera at bench height, shooting from the front.
@Luna left of frame on bench. @Milo right of frame standing, bent toward her.
@Milo's right hand still gripping @Luna's left upper arm. Grey overcast sky behind.

0-5 seconds: @Luna's right hand tightens on @Milo's wrist.
@Luna yanks @Milo's hand off her arm — pulls his wrist outward to the right and releases.
@Luna's body straightens on the bench, shoulders square toward @Milo.
@Luna's chin rises, eyes narrow to slits, jaw clenches hard.
@Luna's lips pull into a thin flat line. @Luna looks directly at @Milo's eyes.
@Milo's released hand drops to his side. @Milo straightens his body upward.
@Milo's mouth opens slightly, eyebrows rise, eyes widen. Caught off guard.
Camera: static medium close-up two-shot.

Sound: fabric ripping from arm grab release, @Milo's hand dropping, @Luna exhaling hard through nose.
[Luna, cold flat female voice, eyes locked on @Milo's eyes, chin raised]: "Stay out of it."
[Milo, low pleading male voice, eyes wide, one hand reaching back toward @Luna]: "Luna."

5-10 seconds: @Luna stands up from the bench. @Luna's full height, body facing @Milo.
@Luna takes one step forward toward @Milo. @Milo takes one step backward.
@Luna's face 30cm from @Milo's face. @Luna's eyes bore into @Milo's eyes.
@Luna's eyebrows lower, jaw juts forward, nostrils flare wide.
@Luna's right hand rises and presses flat against @Milo's chest, fingers spread.
@Luna pushes @Milo back 10cm. @Milo's body rocks backward on his heels.
@Milo's eyes search @Luna's face — brow crumpled, mouth open, lower lip drops.
@Milo's hands hang at his sides, palms open. @Milo does not push back.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to close-up on both faces, shot from @Milo's left side.

Sound: bench creak as @Luna stands, one footstep forward on gravel, palm hitting chest fabric.
[Luna, sharp female voice, hand pressing @Milo's chest, face 30cm from his]: "Let go. Or I'll make you."

10-15 seconds: @Luna turns away from @Milo. @Luna's body rotates left 180 degrees.
@Luna walks away from camera toward background, growing smaller in frame.
@Luna's arms hang at sides, fists clenched, stride fast and rigid.
@Luna's head does not turn back. @Luna's back straight, shoulders locked.
@Milo stands frozen right side of frame, body facing the direction @Luna walked.
@Milo's right hand rises slowly to his own chest where @Luna pushed him.
@Milo's palm flat against his own chest. @Milo's fingers press into his jacket.
@Milo's mouth open, eyes wide, brow pulled up in center, lower lip slack.
@Milo's left hand hangs limp at his side. @Milo stares at @Luna's shrinking figure.
Park bench visible between them, empty now. Leaves on the seat.
Camera: pulls back slowly from close-up to wide shot, both figures separated by the bench.

Lighting: flat grey overcast. No shadows. Muted colors throughout.

Sound: fast footsteps on gravel growing quieter, wind through bare branches,
distant traffic, @Milo's single slow exhale.
No dialogue. Silence holds.
```
