## 클립 1: 어둠 속 배달 (15초)

**키프레임**: `ep03_clip1_alley_delivery.png`
**Scene Reference**: L5_dark_alley
**Element**: Luna

```
High-angle wide shot, camera positioned above and behind, shooting downward into alley.
Narrow alley, wet brick walls on both sides. Dim orange light at the far end.
Puddles on cracked asphalt reflect a faint neon glow from a sign above.

0-5 seconds: @Luna's silhouette appears at top of frame, entering the alley from camera's end.
@Luna in dark coat, body facing away from camera, walking deeper into the alley.
@Luna carries a small brown package tucked under left arm against her ribs.
@Luna's right hand trails along the wet brick wall, fingers brushing the surface.
@Luna's head turns left then right, checking both sides of the alley.
@Luna's shoulders hunched forward, neck pulled down into coat collar.
Camera: static high-angle. @Luna grows smaller walking into depth of frame.

5-10 seconds: Camera cuts to medium shot at ground level, shooting from alley end toward @Luna.
@Luna stops center frame, 2 meters from a figure standing in shadow at background.
Shadowy figure visible only as a dark shape — hands extended palms-up toward @Luna.
@Luna pulls the package from under left arm with right hand.
@Luna extends the package at arm's length toward the figure's hands.
@Luna's face visible: eyes fixed on the figure's hands, brow flat, mouth closed in a straight line.
@Luna's jaw clenched, no expression. @Luna does not look at the figure's face.
The figure's hands take the package. The figure's fingers tremble visibly as they grip the edges.
Camera: static medium shot, @Luna center frame, figure in shadow behind.

10-15 seconds: @Luna releases the package. @Luna drops both hands to her sides.
@Luna takes one step backward, left foot first.
@Luna's eyes stay fixed on the package in the figure's trembling hands for two seconds.
@Luna's lips part slightly, then press back shut. @Luna swallows once.
@Luna turns body 180 degrees to the right, now facing camera.
@Luna's face lit by dim orange light from the alley entrance behind camera.
@Luna's eyes drop to the ground. @Luna's brow creases. @Luna's right hand clenches into a fist at her side.
@Luna begins walking toward camera, head down.
Camera: static medium shot. @Luna walks toward camera from depth of frame.

Lighting: dim orange from alley entrance behind camera. Faint blue-green neon reflection on wet walls.
Deep shadow at alley's far end where the figure stands.

Sound: dripping water echoing off brick walls, footsteps on wet pavement,
package rustling, trembling hands gripping paper, heavy breathing from the figure,
one step backward on wet ground, coat fabric shifting.
No dialogue.
```

## 클립 2: 퇴장과 공포 (10초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep03_clip2_exit_package.png`
**Scene Reference**: L5_dark_alley
**Element**: Luna

```
Medium shot, camera positioned at alley entrance, shooting inward.
@Luna center frame walking toward camera out of the dark alley.
Orange streetlight above and behind camera casts light onto @Luna's approaching face.

0-4 seconds: @Luna walks toward camera from alley depth.
@Luna's body facing camera, head down, eyes on the ground.
@Luna's arms hang at sides, fists clenched, shoulders pulled up tight.
@Luna passes camera on the right side. @Luna's face briefly visible in profile —
@Luna's brow furrowed deep, lips pressed together, eyes narrowed.
@Luna exits frame right.
Camera: static medium shot. @Luna walks past and out of frame.

4-10 seconds: Camera cuts to close-up inside the alley, shooting downward at the figure's hands.
The figure's hands hold the open package at waist level.
Both hands tremble. Fingers grip the torn paper edges.
Contents of the package not visible — only the figure's reaction.
Camera tilts up slowly from hands to the figure's face.
The figure's face enters frame from below: eyes stretched wide open, eyebrows raised to forehead,
mouth hanging open, lower lip trembling, nostrils flared.
The figure's face lit by a faint blue-green glow from below (reflecting off package contents).
The figure's right hand releases the package edge. The figure takes one step backward.
The figure's head shakes once left to right, small and fast.
Camera: slow tilt upward from hands to face, then holds on face.

Lighting: faint blue-green glow from package contents illuminating the figure's face from below.
Deep black shadow behind the figure. No other light source in this section.

Sound: package paper tearing, sharp gasp, one step backward on wet ground,
trembling hands releasing paper, shallow rapid breathing.
No dialogue.
```

```
Medium shot, camera at street level across the road, shooting toward @Luna.
@Luna stands under a single orange streetlight, right side of frame.
@Luna leans back against a wet brick wall, left shoulder blade and head touching the wall.
Rain falls. Orange light pool surrounds @Luna. Darkness beyond the light's edge.

0-5 seconds: @Luna holds phone to right ear with right hand.
@Luna's left hand hangs at her side, fingers opening and closing slowly.
@Luna's face tilted up toward the streetlight, eyes half-closed, lips curved into a forced smile.
@Luna's voice bright but her body rigid — shoulders pressed back against the wall, feet planted wide.
Camera: static medium shot from across the street.

Sound: phone ringing tone, then call connecting click.
[Ash, concerned male voice on phone speaker]: "Sis, you okay?"
[Luna, bright forced female voice, face tilted up, forced smile]: "Yeah. I'm fine."

5-10 seconds: @Luna pulls phone from ear, lowers it to waist height.
@Luna's forced smile drops instantly. @Luna's mouth falls open, corners pulled down.
@Luna's brow crumples, eyes squeeze shut, chin drops to chest.
@Luna slides down the wall 20cm, knees bending slightly, back scraping brick.
@Luna's right hand holding phone drops to her side. Phone screen goes dark.
@Luna's left hand rises to her face, covers her mouth and chin.
@Luna's shoulders shake once. @Luna's breathing visible as chest heaves.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to medium close-up on @Luna against the wall.

10-12 seconds: Extreme close-up of @Luna's hands at her waist, camera 20cm away.
@Luna's right hand holds the dark phone. Both hands tremble visibly.
@Luna's fingers spread wide, then curl inward slowly into tight fists.
The trembling stops. Fists locked. Knuckles white under orange light.
Camera: static extreme close-up of fists.

Sound: shaky breathing steadying, fists tightening against phone case, rain continuing.

12-15 seconds: Camera cuts to medium shot from inside a patrol car, shooting through windshield.
Rain streaks down the glass. Windshield wipers are off.
@Milo sits in the driver's seat, left side of frame, body facing forward.
@Milo's right hand rests on the steering wheel at 12 o'clock.
@Milo's left hand holds a small notepad against the wheel, pen between fingers.
Through the windshield: @Luna visible across the street under the orange streetlight, small in frame.
@Milo's eyes fixed on @Luna through the glass. @Milo does not blink.
@Milo's brow lowered, eyes narrowed, lips pressed flat, jaw muscles flexed.
@Milo's head tilts forward 5 degrees. @Milo's pen taps the notepad twice.
Camera: static, shot from passenger seat toward @Milo and windshield.

Lighting: orange streetlight through rain-streaked windshield. Green dashboard glow on @Milo's hands.
@Milo's face half-lit by orange from outside, half-lit by green from dashboard.

Sound: patrol car engine idling low, radio static crackle from dashboard,
rain hitting car roof, pen tapping notepad twice.
No dialogue. Low frequency hum building. Cut to black.
```
