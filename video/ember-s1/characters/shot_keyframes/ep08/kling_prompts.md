## 클립 1: 증거 소멸 (12초)

**키프레임**: `ep08_s1_hook.png`
**Scene Reference**: L6_police_office
**Element**: Milo

```
Wide shot, camera positioned at back wall of office, shooting forward.
@Milo stands center frame before a large corkboard evidence board.
Board has only scattered pins remaining. Photos and documents stripped away. Empty red strings hang loose.

0-5 seconds: @Milo stands facing the board, back partially toward camera.
@Milo raises right hand and touches the bare corkboard surface.
@Milo's head turns slightly left then right, scanning the empty board.
@Milo steps closer. @Milo's fingers press against a lone remaining pin.
@Milo's eyes wide open, eyebrows raised high, mouth slightly open. Disbelief.
Camera: static wide shot from behind and slightly right of @Milo.
Lighting: harsh white fluorescent tubes overhead, casting flat shadows on corkboard.

5-9 seconds: Cut to medium two-shot. A stern figure in dark uniform enters frame from left.
Figure stops two paces behind @Milo. Figure stands rigid, hands behind back.
@Milo turns body left to face the figure. @Milo's fists clench at both sides.
@Milo's jaw thrust forward, eyes narrowed, nostrils flared.
Camera: static, positioned at right wall, shooting left. Both figures in profile.
Lighting: same harsh fluorescent overhead.

9-12 seconds: The stern figure turns and walks out of frame left.
@Milo stands alone center frame. @Milo's right fist unclenches slowly.
@Milo's shoulders drop. @Milo's eyes close, brow creased deep, mouth pulled down tight.
@Milo turns back toward the empty board. @Milo presses both palms flat against the corkboard.
@Milo's head drops forward, chin to chest.
Camera: static, same position at right wall.
Lighting: harsh fluorescent overhead. Green-gray walls.

Sound: fluorescent light buzzing, hand touching corkboard, sharp intake of breath,
authoritative footsteps approaching, uniform fabric rustling.
[Superior, stern commanding male voice, standing behind Milo]: "The case is closed. Drop it."
@Milo opens his mouth to speak.
[Superior, harder male voice]: "That's an order."
Footsteps walking away. Door closes. Fluorescent buzz only.
```

## 클립 2: 공원 재회와 이별 (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep08_s3_park_reunion.png`
**Scene Reference**: L4_park_bench
**Element**: Luna + Milo

```
Wide shot, camera positioned 10 meters in front of a park bench under a large tree.
@Luna sits on the bench left side, body facing right, hands folded on lap.
Fallen brown and yellow leaves scattered on the ground around the bench.
Afternoon sunlight filtering through branches, casting leaf shadows on the path.

0-3 seconds: @Milo walks into frame from background right along a gravel path.
@Milo's steps are slow and heavy. @Milo's hands hang at sides.
@Milo's face flat, eyes steady and unblinking, lips pressed together.
@Luna looks up at @Milo approaching. @Luna's lips part slightly, eyebrows lift.
Camera: static wide shot.
Lighting: warm golden afternoon sun from upper left. Tree shadow covering the bench.

3-7 seconds: @Milo stops in front of the bench, standing, body facing @Luna.
@Luna remains seated, looking up at @Milo's face.
@Luna's brow furrowed, lips pressed together, eyes scanning @Milo's expression.
@Milo looks down at @Luna. @Milo's eyes soft, brow wrinkled, mouth corners pulled slightly down.
@Milo does not sit. @Milo's hands stay at his sides.
Camera: slow push-in from wide to medium two-shot, shooting from left side at 30 degrees.
Lighting: warm golden sun through leaves on @Milo's face. @Luna in dappled tree shadow.

7-12 seconds: Cut to close-up two-shot from side angle.
@Milo's face visible left of frame, @Luna's face right of frame, looking up.
@Milo's eyes fill with tears. @Milo's chin trembles once. @Milo blinks, looks away right.
@Luna's eyes wide, eyebrows rising, mouth opening slightly. Recognition of what is happening.
@Luna reaches right hand forward toward @Milo. @Milo steps back one pace.
@Luna's hand freezes in the air between them.
Camera: static close-up, shot from left side. Both faces in near-profile.
Lighting: warm golden sun on @Milo's face, tree shadow on @Luna's face.

12-15 seconds: @Milo turns body away from @Luna, facing the sunlit gravel path.
@Milo's back toward camera and toward @Luna. @Milo takes one step forward.
@Milo does not look back. @Milo walks slowly along the path toward bright sunlight.
@Luna's hand drops to the bench surface. @Luna's mouth opens but no sound comes out.
@Luna's eyes wet, brow crumpled, lips trembling. @Luna watches @Milo's back receding.
Camera: static, @Luna seated in foreground right, @Milo walking away into background center.
Lighting: @Milo walks into bright golden backlight. @Luna remains in tree shadow.

Sound: birds chirping, leaves rustling, slow footsteps on gravel path,
quiet breathing, hand touching wooden bench.
[Milo, quiet sad male voice, standing in front of Luna, looking down at her face]:
"I thought you were the kindest person I knew."
Long silence. Footsteps walking away on gravel, growing distant.
```

## 클립 3: 혼자 남은 벤치 (10초)

**키프레임**: `ep08_s5_end.png`
**Scene Reference**: L4_park_bench
**Element**: Luna

```
Medium shot, camera positioned 3 meters in front of the bench, shooting straight on.
@Luna sits alone on the park bench center frame, under the large tree.
Deep tree shadow covers @Luna entirely. Bright afternoon sunlight beyond the tree on the path.

0-4 seconds: @Luna sits motionless. @Luna's hands rest flat on the bench surface, palms down.
@Luna's head tilted slightly down, eyes fixed on the gravel path where @Milo walked away.
@Luna's face blank, eyes steady and unblinking, lips flat. No expression.
A single white petal detaches from the tree above and drifts down.
The petal lands on @Luna's left thigh. @Luna does not react.
Camera: static medium shot.
Lighting: deep tree shadow on @Luna. Bright golden sunlight on the empty path behind.

4-7 seconds: A phone on the bench beside @Luna's right hand lights up and buzzes.
Screen text visible: "ASH — 7 missed calls."
@Luna's eyes move right, looking down at the phone screen.
@Luna's right hand does not move toward the phone. Phone buzzes again.
@Luna's jaw clenches once. @Luna's eyes close slowly. @Luna exhales through slightly open mouth.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to close-up on @Luna's face and the phone beside her hand.
Lighting: phone screen glow on @Luna's right hand. Tree shadow on face.

7-10 seconds: Phone screen goes dark. @Luna opens eyes.
@Luna looks straight ahead at the empty path. @Luna's face still, lips closed, eyes half-lidded.
@Luna's left hand curls slowly into a fist on the bench surface.
Wind moves through tree branches above. Leaves shift. Light and shadow ripple across @Luna's face.
Camera: holds close-up on @Luna's face, shot straight on.
Lighting: shifting dappled tree shadow and golden sunlight across @Luna's face.

Sound: phone buzzing repeatedly, birdsong, wind through leaves,
quiet breathing, wood creaking under shifting weight.
No dialogue.
```
