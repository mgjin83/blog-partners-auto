## 클립 1: Hook + Instructions (12초)

**키프레임**: `ep07_s1_hook.png`
**Scene Reference**: L2_duchess_club
**Element**: Luna + Duchess

```
Extreme close-up of a dark polished table surface, center frame.
Cold blue neon reflections streak across the glossy black surface.
Camera positioned at table height, shooting across the table surface at a low angle.

0-4 seconds: @Duchess's right hand enters frame from the right.
@Duchess's hand adorned with two silver rings and a thin bracelet. Nails painted dark red.
@Duchess's fingers rest on top of a thick manila envelope. @Duchess pushes the envelope slowly across the table from right to left.
The envelope slides across the polished surface. Neon reflections ripple around the envelope as it moves.
@Duchess's hand lifts off the envelope and withdraws from frame to the right.
The envelope sits alone center frame on the dark table.
Camera: static extreme close-up at table level.
Lighting: cold blue neon from ceiling strips reflected on table surface. No warm light.

4-8 seconds: Cut to medium two-shot. Camera positioned at the end of the table, shooting lengthwise.
@Duchess sits on a leather sofa, right side of frame, body angled left toward the table.
@Duchess leans back against the sofa. Left arm draped along the sofa back. Right hand holds a wine glass at chest height.
@Duchess's chin raised slightly, eyes half-lidded, one corner of mouth raised. @Duchess looks down at @Luna across the table.
@Luna sits in a straight-backed chair, left side of frame, body facing right toward @Duchess.
@Luna's back straight. Both hands on her knees. @Luna looks down at the manila envelope on the table between them.
@Luna's face blank, lips flat, eyes fixed on the envelope.
Deep shadows between them. Table between them with the envelope center frame.
Camera: static medium two-shot.
Lighting: cold blue neon from ceiling. @Duchess's face lit from upper right. @Luna's face lit from upper left. Deep shadow in the space between them.

8-12 seconds: @Luna leans forward. @Luna's right hand reaches across the table.
@Luna picks up the manila envelope with both hands. @Luna holds it at chest level.
@Luna turns the envelope over once, looking at both sides. @Luna looks up from the envelope at @Duchess.
@Luna's eyebrows pulled together slightly, lips pressed flat, eyes steady on @Duchess's face.
@Duchess does not move. @Duchess watches @Luna over the rim of the wine glass. @Duchess takes one slow sip.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to close medium on both figures.
Lighting: cold blue neon. Wine glass catches a blue neon reflection.

Sound: envelope sliding across polished surface, leather creaking, wine glass lifted, quiet club bass vibrating through floor.
[Duchess, casual smooth female voice, chin raised, eyes on Luna]: "Deliver this. It ends someone's problem."
Silence. Envelope paper rustling as @Luna handles it.
```

## 클립 2: Refusal + Discovery (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep07_s3_refusal.png`
**Scene Reference**: L2_duchess_club
**Element**: Luna + Duchess

```
Close-up of @Luna's face, center frame. Camera 60cm from @Luna's face, shooting straight at her.
@Luna looks down at documents pulled from the manila envelope. Documents held in both hands at chest level.

0-5 seconds: @Luna's eyes move left to right, reading the documents. @Luna turns one page with her right hand.
@Luna's eyes widen. @Luna's eyebrows rise sharply. @Luna's mouth opens 1cm.
@Luna reads further. @Luna's expression changes: eyebrows lower, eyes narrow, jaw clenches.
@Luna's lips press into a thin hard line. @Luna's nostrils flare with one sharp exhale.
@Luna looks up from the documents. @Luna looks directly at @Duchess, off-screen to the right.
@Luna shakes her head once, left to right. A single sharp movement.
Camera: static close-up on @Luna's face.
Lighting: cold blue neon from above. @Luna's green eyes reflect blue light.

Sound: paper turning, sharp exhale through nose.
[Luna, firm female voice, eyes on Duchess, jaw clenched]: "No."

5-10 seconds: Cut to close-up of @Duchess's face, center frame. Camera 60cm from @Duchess, shooting straight at her.
@Duchess's smile disappears. @Duchess's lips flatten. @Duchess's chin drops 2cm.
@Duchess's eyes open wider, unblinking, locked on @Luna off-screen to the left.
@Duchess sets wine glass down on the sofa arm with her left hand without looking at it.
@Duchess's right hand rises to chest level, index finger extended, pointing at @Luna.
@Duchess's jaw shifts forward. @Duchess's eyes narrow to slits. @Duchess's nostrils widen.
Camera: static close-up on @Duchess's face.
Lighting: cold blue neon from above and right. Deep shadow under @Duchess's brow. Eyes catch blue neon.

[Duchess, ice-cold female voice, smile gone, finger pointing at Luna]: "Then your brother's protection ends tonight."
Long silence. @Duchess holds the pointing finger steady. @Duchess does not blink.

10-15 seconds: Cut to extreme close-up of documents in @Luna's hands, center frame.
Camera positioned 15cm from the paper, shooting straight down at the page.
A name is visible on the document. @Luna's thumbs hold the page edges.
@Luna's right thumb moves. A phone screen slides into frame from the right edge, held in @Luna's right hand.
@Luna positions the phone above the document. The phone screen shows a camera viewfinder.
Phone camera shutter fires. Screen flashes white for one frame.
@Luna's left hand turns to the next page. Phone camera fires again. Another white flash.
@Luna's hands tremble. @Luna slides the phone out of frame to the right.
Cut to close-up of @Luna's face from the left side. @Luna's mouth corners pulled down, brow crumpled, eyes wet and red-rimmed.
@Luna's chin drops to her chest. @Luna's eyes squeeze shut for two seconds then open.
@Luna's eyes look down at the documents. Green eyes reflecting blue neon, liquid pooling at lower lids.
Camera: push-in from extreme close-up of documents to close-up of @Luna's face.
Lighting: cold blue neon. Phone screen glow on @Luna's hands and chin. Blue neon reflection in @Luna's wet eyes.

Sound: paper turning, phone camera shutter click twice (barely audible), sharp breath in, swallowing.
No dialogue. Silence except for paper and breathing.
```

## 클립 3: End — Rain Walk (10초)

**키프레임**: `ep07_s5_end.png`
**Scene Reference**: L3_rainy_street
**Element**: Luna

```
Medium shot of a club exit door, center frame. Heavy rain falling.
Camera positioned across the street, shooting at the door from 5 meters away.
Wet pavement in foreground. Rain visible as white streaks against dark background.

0-4 seconds: The club door opens outward. @Luna steps out through the door into the rain.
@Luna stands on the sidewalk, center frame, body facing the camera. Both arms at her sides.
@Luna holds the manila envelope in her left hand, pressed against her left thigh.
@Luna's right hand hangs at her side. Phone bulge visible in @Luna's right coat pocket.
Rain hits @Luna's head and shoulders. @Luna's hair flattens against her forehead within two seconds.
@Luna does not move. @Luna stands still. @Luna's face tilted up 10 degrees toward the sky.
@Luna's eyes closed. Lips parted slightly. Rain runs down @Luna's cheeks and chin.
Camera: static medium shot from across the street.
Lighting: cold blue neon from the club sign above the door, casting blue on @Luna's left side. Orange streetlight from upper right of frame. Rain streaks lit white by the streetlight.

4-7 seconds: @Luna lowers her face. @Luna opens her eyes. @Luna looks straight ahead past the camera.
@Luna's jaw tightens. @Luna's eyes narrow. @Luna's brow lowers. Lips press together.
@Luna takes one step forward with her right foot. Then another with her left.
@Luna walks toward the camera. @Luna's right hand reaches into her coat pocket, fingers wrap around the phone inside.
@Luna's left hand holds the envelope at her side. The envelope darkens as rain soaks the paper.
Camera: static. @Luna walks closer to camera, from medium shot toward close medium.
Lighting: @Luna walks from blue club neon into the space between lights. Orange streetlight from upper right catches rain on @Luna's right shoulder.

7-10 seconds: @Luna continues walking forward. @Luna passes the camera on the left side.
Camera does not move. @Luna's figure moves past and away from camera.
@Luna's back visible, walking away into the rain-soaked street. @Luna center frame growing smaller.
Neon reflections in puddles on both sides of the street — red, blue, yellow fragments of light.
@Luna's silhouette darkens as she moves further from the lights. Envelope visible in left hand.
Rain continues heavy. @Luna does not look back. @Luna walks steady, shoulders squared.
Camera: static. @Luna walks away from camera into depth of frame.
Lighting: orange streetlight from behind camera casts @Luna's long shadow forward on the wet pavement. Neon puddle reflections on both sides.

Sound: heavy rain on pavement, club door shutting behind, footsteps on wet concrete — steady rhythm, distant traffic, water running in gutters.
No dialogue. Cut to black.
```
