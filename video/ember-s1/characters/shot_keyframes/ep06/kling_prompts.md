## 클립 1: Hook + Receipt Discovery (15초)

**키프레임**: `ep06_s1_hook.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop_dark
**Element**: Luna

```
Wide shot of a dark flower shop interior at night. All lights off.
Pale blue moonlight enters through a large front window, left side of frame.
Silhouettes of flower arrangements on shelves along back wall. Flower stems and leaves cast long thin shadows on the floor.
Camera positioned at the front door, shooting toward the back of the shop.

0-5 seconds: @Luna stands behind the counter, background center frame, body facing left.
@Luna pulls an apron over her head with both hands. @Luna folds the apron and sets it on the counter.
@Luna's face barely visible in moonlight. Lips closed, eyes downcast, looking at the counter surface.
A jacket hangs over the back of a wooden chair next to the counter, right side of frame.
@Luna turns body right toward the chair. @Luna reaches right hand toward the jacket.
@Luna picks up the jacket with both hands. @Luna holds the jacket in front of her body.
Camera: static wide shot.
Lighting: pale blue moonlight from the front window on the left. No artificial light. Deep blue shadows throughout.

5-10 seconds: Cut to close-up. Camera at counter height, 60cm from @Luna's hands, shooting upward at 20 degrees.
@Luna's hands hold the jacket at chest level, center frame. @Luna folds the jacket over her left arm.
A small crumpled piece of paper falls from the jacket pocket. The paper tumbles and lands on the counter surface.
@Luna's right hand stops. @Luna's fingers open. @Luna looks down at the paper on the counter.
@Luna's right hand picks up the crumpled paper. @Luna unfolds it with both hands slowly.
Close-up of @Luna's face from below. Moonlight catches @Luna's eyes as she reads.
@Luna's eyes widen. @Luna's eyebrows rise. @Luna's lips part 1cm. @Luna stops breathing.
@Luna's hands begin to tremble. The paper shakes visibly between her fingers.
Camera: slow push-in from close-up of hands to close-up of @Luna's face.
Lighting: pale blue moonlight on the paper and @Luna's face from the left. Right side of face in shadow.

10-15 seconds: @Luna holds the receipt closer to her face. Both hands gripping the paper edges.
@Luna's eyes move left to right, reading. @Luna's mouth opens wider.
@Luna's right hand moves to cover her mouth. @Luna's left hand holds the receipt at arm's length.
@Luna's brow crumples upward in the center. @Luna's chin pushes forward and trembles.
@Luna's eyes fill with liquid. Moonlight reflects off the wet surface of @Luna's eyes.
@Luna's shoulders pull inward. @Luna's chest contracts with one sharp inhale.
Camera: static close-up on @Luna's face, shot from the front.
Lighting: pale blue moonlight from the left window. Flower silhouettes cast shadows across @Luna's right cheek.

Sound: fabric rustling, paper falling on wood, paper uncrumpling, sharp intake of breath, hands trembling against paper.
No dialogue.
```

## 클립 2: Receipt Close-up + Weight of Truth (13초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep06_s3_receipt_closeup.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop_dark
**Element**: Luna

```
Extreme close-up of a crumpled receipt held by two trembling hands, center frame.
Camera positioned 20cm from the paper, shooting straight at its surface.
Pale blue moonlight illuminates the paper.

0-4 seconds: The receipt fills the frame. Visible on the paper: a date, a currency amount, a lender's stamp.
@Luna's fingertips grip the paper edges. @Luna's thumbnails are white from pressing hard.
The paper shakes with @Luna's trembling. Small creases in the paper catch light and shadow.
Camera: static extreme close-up.
Lighting: pale blue moonlight from upper left. Paper surface bright. @Luna's fingers in half-shadow.

Sound: paper trembling, quiet breathing through nose.

4-8 seconds: Cut to medium shot. Camera positioned at floor level, shooting upward at 30 degrees.
@Luna sinks down from standing. @Luna's knees bend. @Luna slides down against the front of the counter.
@Luna sits on the floor, back against the counter, center frame. Legs bent in front of her.
@Luna holds the receipt in her right hand, resting on her right knee. @Luna's left hand lies palm-up on the floor.
Flower silhouettes surround @Luna on both sides. Moonlight casts long flower-stem shadows across @Luna's body.
@Luna's head tilts back against the counter. @Luna's eyes stare upward at the ceiling.
@Luna's mouth corners pulled down, brow crumpled, eyes wet and red-rimmed.
Camera: static, floor-level shot.
Lighting: pale blue moonlight from the front window. @Luna's face half-lit, half in shadow. Flower shadows stripe across her legs and arms.

8-13 seconds: @Luna lowers her head forward. @Luna looks down at the receipt in her right hand.
@Luna brings the receipt to her chest with both hands. @Luna presses the paper against her sternum.
Tears run down @Luna's cheeks. Two lines of liquid catching moonlight on each side of her face.
@Luna's eyes squeeze shut. @Luna's mouth opens. @Luna's breath comes in two short gasps.
@Luna's shoulders shake twice. @Luna curls forward, head dropping toward her knees.
@Luna's fingers crumple the receipt tighter against her chest.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to close-up on @Luna's face, shot from the right side.
Lighting: moonlight from the left catches tears on @Luna's left cheek. Right side of face dark.

Sound: quiet sobbing, paper crumpling in grip, a single flower petal falling from a shelf and landing on the floor, breathing through tears.
[Luna, broken whisper female voice, head curled down, face toward the receipt]: "It was for me..."
Silence. Sobbing continues.
```

## 클립 3: End — Decision (10초)

**키프레임**: `ep06_s5_end.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop_dark
**Element**: Luna

```
Close-up of @Luna sitting on the dark flower shop floor, back against the counter, center frame.
Camera at floor level, 1 meter from @Luna, shooting straight at her face.

0-4 seconds: @Luna sits motionless. Head bowed forward. Receipt crumpled in both hands at her chest.
Tears dry on @Luna's cheeks. Streaks of reflected moonlight on the dried tear lines.
A single petal detaches from a wilting flower on the shelf above @Luna.
The petal drifts downward slowly. The petal lands on @Luna's left hand resting on her knee.
@Luna does not move. @Luna's eyes remain closed. The petal sits on her still hand.
Camera: static close-up.
Lighting: pale blue moonlight shifts slowly across the floor from left to right. Shadow edge moves across @Luna's face.

4-7 seconds: @Luna opens her eyes. @Luna looks down at the petal on her hand.
@Luna's eyes red-rimmed and swollen. @Luna's brow pulls together.
@Luna closes her right hand slowly around the crumpled receipt. @Luna's knuckles whiten.
@Luna lifts her head. @Luna looks straight ahead, past the camera.
@Luna's jaw tightens. @Luna's lips press together into a thin hard line. @Luna's eyes narrow.
@Luna swallows once. @Luna's nostrils flare with one slow exhale.
Camera: static, same position.
Lighting: moonlight now centered on @Luna's face. Both eyes visible. Flower shadows across her forehead.

7-10 seconds: @Luna places her left hand flat on the floor. @Luna pushes herself upward.
@Luna rises to standing. @Luna's right hand holds the crumpled receipt at her side.
@Luna stands behind the counter, body facing the front window, center frame.
@Luna's silhouette dark against the pale moonlit window. @Luna's shoulders squared. Head level.
@Luna's right hand slides the receipt into her back pocket.
Camera: pulls back slowly from close-up to medium shot, floor level to chest level.
Lighting: @Luna becomes a dark silhouette against the pale blue moonlit window. No detail visible on her face.

Sound: petal landing softly on skin, breathing steadying, fabric rustling as @Luna stands, receipt paper sliding into pocket, quiet night outside.
No dialogue. Cut to black.
```
