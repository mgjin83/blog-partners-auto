## 클립 1: 마지막 거래 (15초)

**키프레임**: `ep10_s1_hook.png`
**Scene Reference**: L2_duchess_club
**Element**: Luna + Duchess

```
Medium shot, camera positioned outside the club entrance, shooting toward the door.
@Luna stands center frame before the club entrance at night. Neon sign above the door, reflecting in rain puddles on the ground.
@Luna wears a dark coat, right hand inside coat pocket. Rain falls on @Luna's shoulders and hair.

0-4 seconds: @Luna faces the club door, body facing forward. @Luna's green eyes focused straight ahead.
@Luna's lips closed, jaw set, eyes narrowed slightly. @Luna's left hand hangs at side, fingers curled.
@Luna inhales through nose. @Luna's shoulders rise and drop once.
@Luna steps forward and pushes through the door with left hand. Door opens inward.
Camera: static medium shot. @Luna walks toward camera and past, into the club behind.
Lighting: cold blue and pink neon from club sign above. Orange reflection in rain puddles. Dark night sky.

4-10 seconds: Cut to interior. Medium two-shot, camera positioned at right wall, shooting left.
@Duchess sits on a dark leather sofa left side of frame, body angled right. Legs crossed.
@Duchess holds a wine glass in left hand at chest height. A thick envelope sits on the low table in front of @Duchess.
@Duchess's face: one corner of mouth raised, eyes half-lidded, chin tilted slightly up.
@Luna stands right side of frame, body facing @Duchess. @Luna's right hand still inside coat pocket.
@Duchess pushes the envelope across the table toward @Luna with right hand.
@Luna looks down at the envelope. @Luna's face blank, eyes steady and unblinking, lips flat.
@Luna reaches down with left hand and picks up the envelope. @Luna slides it inside her coat with left hand.
@Luna's right hand shifts inside the coat pocket. Small movement. @Duchess does not notice.
@Duchess lifts wine glass, takes a sip, sets it down on table.
Camera: static medium two-shot.
Lighting: cold blue neon strips along club walls. Deep shadows on faces. Purple ambient glow from ceiling.

10-15 seconds: @Luna stands upright, body still facing @Duchess.
@Luna looks at @Duchess's face. @Luna's eyes narrow for one moment, then go blank again.
@Duchess looks up at @Luna from the sofa. @Duchess's lips curve into a slow smile, eyes half-lidded, chin raised.
@Luna turns body away from @Duchess. @Luna walks toward the exit, back to camera.
Chair scrapes on floor. @Luna's coat sways as she walks.
@Duchess remains seated, watching @Luna's back. @Duchess's smile fades. @Duchess picks up wine glass again.
Camera: static, @Duchess in foreground left on sofa, @Luna walking away toward background center.
Lighting: cold blue neon. @Luna's figure darkening as she moves toward the dim exit.

Sound: rain falling on pavement outside, @Luna's footsteps on wet ground, door opening,
interior club: low bass hum, envelope sliding across table, wine glass set down,
fabric movement inside coat pocket, chair scraping, footsteps walking away.
[Duchess, satisfied smooth female voice, seated on sofa, pushing envelope]:
"Last one. Then we're even."
[Luna, steady quiet female voice, standing, looking down at envelope]:
"Even."
```

## 클립 2: 증거 투하 (7초)

**키프레임**: `ep10_s3_evidence_delivery.png`
**Scene Reference**: L3_rainy_street
**Element**: Luna

```
Close-up, camera positioned at waist height, shooting straight at @Luna's hands and the mailbox.
@Luna stands in front of a blue metal mailbox on a dark street. Rain falls steadily.
A single streetlight above casts orange glow on the mailbox and @Luna's hands.

0-3 seconds: @Luna's right hand reaches inside her coat. @Luna pulls out a thick brown envelope.
The envelope is damp from rain. A police station address partially visible on the front, handwritten.
@Luna holds the envelope with both hands in front of the mailbox slot.
@Luna's thumbs press against the envelope surface. @Luna's fingers are steady. No trembling.
Camera: static close-up on hands, envelope, and mailbox slot.
Lighting: single orange streetlight from above. Rain streaks visible in the light. Dark street background.

3-7 seconds: @Luna pushes the envelope into the metal slot with both thumbs.
Envelope slides halfway in. @Luna pushes once more. Envelope disappears inside.
Metal flap swings shut with a clang.
@Luna's hands remain on the mailbox surface for one moment. Rain hits her knuckles.
@Luna pulls hands back. @Luna's fingers curl into loose fists at her sides.
Cut to medium shot: @Luna stands facing the mailbox, head slightly bowed.
@Luna's face in orange streetlight: eyes closed briefly, shoulders drop, exhale through slightly open mouth.
@Luna opens eyes. @Luna turns away from the mailbox and walks out of frame right.
Camera: static medium shot from across the narrow street.
Lighting: single orange streetlight on @Luna and mailbox. Rain falling through the light cone. Dark everywhere else.

Sound: steady rain on pavement, paper and hands rustling, envelope sliding into metal slot,
metal flap clanging shut, rain on knuckles, @Luna's exhale, footsteps walking away on wet pavement.
No dialogue.
```

## 클립 3: 붕괴 몽타주 (12초)

**Scene Reference**: L6_police_office
**Element**: Milo + Duchess + Luna

```
MONTAGE — four segments, quick cuts.

0-3 seconds — SEGMENT A (Police Office):
Medium shot, camera at desk level, shooting across at @Milo.
@Milo sits at a metal desk center frame in a police office.
@Milo holds a thick brown envelope, tearing it open with both hands.
CCTV screenshots and documents spill out across the desk surface.
@Milo picks up one photo with right hand, holds it close to his face.
@Milo's eyes widen, eyebrows shoot up, mouth drops open. @Milo's left hand grips desk edge.
Camera: static medium shot.
Lighting: harsh white fluorescent overhead. Gray office walls.
Sound: envelope ripping, papers scattering on metal desk, sharp intake of breath.

3-6 seconds — SEGMENT B (Duchess Club):
Wide shot, camera at street level, shooting toward the club entrance.
Police officers in dark uniforms rush through the club door.
Flashlight beams sweep through blue darkness inside.
Neon signs on the walls flicker and go dark one by one.
Silhouettes of officers moving through the interior.
No characters in Element visible — all anonymous figures.
Camera: static wide shot.
Lighting: flashlight beams cutting through blue-black interior. Red and blue police lights reflecting off wet exterior walls.
Sound: doors breached, shouting, neon buzzing and dying, radio chatter, boots on floor.

6-9 seconds — SEGMENT C (Club Exterior):
Medium shot, camera at street level, shooting toward the club exit.
@Duchess is led out of the club by two officers, one on each side gripping her arms.
@Duchess's platinum blonde hair lit by cold exterior light and red-blue police flashers.
@Duchess's eyes wide, eyebrows raised, mouth open. @Duchess pulls against the officers' grip.
@Duchess's head turns back toward the club door. @Duchess's jaw clenches, eyes narrow.
Camera: static medium shot.
Lighting: red and blue police lights alternating on @Duchess's face. Cold white street light from above.
Sound: handcuffs clicking, police radio crackling, @Duchess's sharp breath, boots on wet pavement.

9-12 seconds — SEGMENT D (Flower Shop):
Medium shot, camera at counter level, shooting at @Luna from across the counter.
@Luna stands behind the counter in a flower shop in warm daylight. Blinds open. Bright morning light.
@Luna holds a phone in right hand at waist level, looking down at the screen.
Phone screen shows a news headline. @Luna reads it.
@Luna's face: lips closed, eyes steady and unblinking, no movement. Completely still.
@Luna sets the phone face-down on the counter with right hand. Quiet tap.
@Luna picks up scissors with right hand. @Luna resumes cutting a flower stem. Normal motion.
Camera: static medium shot.
Lighting: warm amber morning light from open window. Soft shadows on counter.
Sound: phone notification chime, quiet tap of phone on counter, scissors cutting stem.
No dialogue in any segment.
```

## 클립 4: 재개와 잔불 (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep10_s5_reopening.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop
**Element**: Luna

```
Medium shot, camera positioned at the shop entrance, shooting inward toward the counter.
@Luna stands behind the counter center frame. Warm daylight fills the shop. Flowers arranged on shelves and in vases.

0-4 seconds: A female customer enters from foreground, walking past camera toward the counter.
Customer stops in front of the counter, body facing @Luna. Customer points at a bouquet on the right shelf.
@Luna nods once. @Luna reaches right and lifts the bouquet with both hands. @Luna sets it on the counter.
@Luna pulls wrapping paper from under the counter. @Luna begins wrapping the flowers.
@Luna's face: lips closed with slight smile, eyes soft, cheeks relaxed. Practiced and smooth.
Customer watches @Luna wrap. Customer smiles.
Camera: static medium shot from entrance.
Lighting: warm amber daylight from shop window behind camera. Soft golden glow on flowers and @Luna's hands.

4-8 seconds: Cut to extreme close-up of @Luna's hands wrapping the bouquet.
@Luna's fingers fold paper around stems with even pressure. No hesitation.
@Luna's right hand ties ribbon around the wrapped stems. Left hand holds paper in place.
@Luna's hands are perfectly steady. No trembling. Mechanical precision.
@Luna's fingers move through each step without pause. Scissors cut ribbon end clean.
Camera: static extreme close-up, overhead angle, shooting down at hands and flowers.
Lighting: warm amber from above. Soft shadows of @Luna's fingers on the white wrapping paper.

8-10 seconds: Cut back to medium shot.
@Luna hands the wrapped bouquet to the customer with both hands across the counter.
Customer takes the bouquet. Customer's mouth moves.
@Luna's lips form a calm practiced smile. @Luna's eyes crinkle slightly at corners. Head tilts right.
Customer turns and walks toward the exit past camera.
@Luna watches the customer leave. @Luna's smile holds until the customer passes the door.
Camera: static medium shot from entrance.
Lighting: warm amber daylight.

Sound: door bell chiming, footsteps on tile floor, flower stems being cut,
paper wrapping and folding, ribbon being tied, scissors snipping.
[Customer, gentle female voice, pointing at bouquet]: "These are beautiful."
[Luna, calm serene female voice, handing bouquet with both hands]: "Thank you."

10-15 seconds: Cut to wide exterior shot from across the street at night.
@Luna pulls down the metal shop shutter with both hands. Shutter rattles to the ground.
@Luna turns away from the shop, body facing the street. @Luna stands alone under a single streetlight.
Empty street stretches left and right. No one visible.
@Luna begins walking right along the sidewalk. @Luna's hands in coat pockets. Head slightly down.
@Luna's face: lips flat, eyes forward, expression still.
Across the street in deep shadow, a small orange ember glows. A cigarette.
A dark figure stands in the shadow of a building wall, facing @Luna's direction. Figure does not move.
@Luna's steps slow. @Luna's head turns left toward the orange glow across the street.
@Luna's face in streetlight: eyes steady, no widening, no narrowing. @Luna sees it.
@Luna turns head forward again. @Luna continues walking. Steps resume normal pace.
The orange ember glows brighter for a moment, then dims.
Camera: static wide shot from 15 meters. @Luna on right sidewalk, orange ember across the street on left.
Lighting: single white streetlight on @Luna. Dark blue night everywhere else. Orange ember point of light in shadow.

Sound: shutter rattling down, footsteps on wet pavement, distant city hum,
cigarette ember crackling softly in darkness.
@Luna's footsteps pause two beats. Then resume. Growing distant.
No dialogue. Cut to black.
```
