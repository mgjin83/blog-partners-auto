## 클립 1: 대면과 분노 (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep09_s1_hook.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop_dark
**Element**: Luna + Ash

```
Medium shot, camera positioned at right wall, shooting left toward the shop door.
@Luna stands behind the counter left side of frame, body facing right, head tilted down.
@Luna holds flower stems in both hands. Scissors on the counter beside her.
Closed blinds on window. Thin strips of daylight cut through slats, casting horizontal bars of light across flowers and counter.

0-3 seconds: @Luna arranges flowers in the striped light. @Luna's face relaxed, lips closed, eyes focused down on stems.
Door at background center bursts open. Bright daylight floods in from outside.
@Ash's silhouette stands in the doorway, body facing forward into the shop.
@Luna's head snaps up. @Luna's eyes wide open, eyebrows raised, hands freeze mid-cut.
@Ash steps forward into the shop. Door swings shut behind @Ash. Light bars return.
Camera: static medium shot.
Lighting: thin horizontal daylight bars through blinds. Burst of bright backlight from open door, then dim again.

3-8 seconds: @Ash walks toward the counter, stopping one meter from @Luna. @Ash stands right side of frame.
@Ash's face visible in striped light: nostrils flared, eyebrows pinched tight, jaw thrust forward.
@Ash's hands at sides, fists clenched. @Ash's hazel eyes locked on @Luna's face.
@Luna sets scissors down on counter with right hand. @Luna's body straightens upright.
@Luna looks directly at @Ash's face. @Luna's eyes half-lidded, brow slightly furrowed, lips closed. Exhaustion.
Flowers on the counter between them. Light bars cross both figures horizontally.
Camera: slow push-in from medium to medium close-up, shooting from right side at 45 degrees.
Lighting: thin daylight bars through blinds across both faces. Dim warm amber from a small lamp on counter.

8-15 seconds: @Ash leans forward, both hands flat on counter surface, arms rigid.
@Ash's face 50cm from @Luna's face. @Ash's eyes burning wide, brow creased deep, mouth open.
@Luna does not step back. @Luna's chin lifts slightly. @Luna's eyes narrow, lips press flat.
@Ash's right hand slams the counter surface. Flower petals scatter.
@Luna flinches but holds position. @Luna's jaw clenches once.
@Ash pulls back, stands upright, runs both hands through hair. @Ash's chin drops, eyes squeeze shut.
Camera: holds medium close-up, both faces visible, shot from right side.
Lighting: daylight bars through blinds. Dim amber lamp on counter between them.

Sound: quiet flower arranging, then door slamming open violently, daylight rushing in,
footsteps approaching on tile floor, scissors set down on counter, hand slamming counter,
petals scattering, heavy breathing.
[Ash, angry desperate male voice, leaning forward over counter, face toward Luna]:
"I know everything. What you've been doing."
Silence. @Luna says nothing.
[Ash, louder shaking male voice, right hand slamming counter]:
"I never asked you to do that!"
[Luna, quiet calm female voice, looking directly at Ash's face]:
"You did the same thing."
[Ash, voice breaking, eyes squeezed shut]:
"That's different!"
[Luna, steady quiet female voice, chin lifted, eyes narrowed]:
"How?"
Silence. Only breathing.
```

## 클립 2: 침묵과 퇴장 (14초)

**키프레임**: `ep09_s3_silence.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop_dark
**Element**: Luna + Ash

```
Close-up two-shot, camera positioned at left wall, shooting right.
@Ash stands right side of frame, @Luna behind counter left side of frame.
Both faces visible in the thin daylight bars from blinds.

0-5 seconds: @Ash stands still, body facing @Luna. @Ash's fists slowly unclench.
@Ash's eyes fill with tears. @Ash's lower lip trembles. @Ash's brow crumples upward.
@Ash blinks. Two tears track down @Ash's left cheek through a bar of light.
@Luna watches @Ash's face. @Luna's brow furrowed tight, lips pressed together.
@Luna extends left hand slowly across the counter toward @Ash, palm up.
@Ash looks down at @Luna's outstretched hand. @Ash steps back one pace from the counter.
@Luna's hand stays extended over the counter surface. @Luna's fingers curl slightly inward.
Camera: static close-up, both faces and @Luna's extended hand visible.
Lighting: thin daylight bars through blinds. One bar of light crossing @Ash's wet cheek.

5-10 seconds: @Ash turns body away from @Luna, facing the door at background center.
@Ash's back toward camera. @Ash's shoulders hunched forward, head down.
@Ash walks toward the door. Steps slow and heavy on tile floor.
@Luna pulls her hand back to her side. @Luna's hand grips the counter edge. Knuckles white.
@Luna's mouth corners pulled down, brow crumpled, eyes wet and red-rimmed.
@Ash reaches the door. @Ash's right hand grips the door handle. @Ash pauses.
@Ash's head turns slightly right, quarter-profile visible. @Ash does not look back fully.
@Ash's jaw clenched, eyes fixed forward on the door, brow creased.
Camera: medium shot from behind and left of @Luna, @Ash at the door in background center.
Lighting: thin daylight bars across the shop floor. Bright line of light visible under the closed door.

10-14 seconds: @Ash pushes the door open. Bright daylight floods in from outside.
@Ash steps through the doorway into the light. @Ash's silhouette darkens against the bright exterior.
Door swings shut slowly. Light narrows to a sliver, then gone. Shop falls back into dim striped light.
@Luna stands behind the counter, body facing the closed door. @Luna does not move.
@Luna's face in striped light: eyes wide, staring at the closed door, lips slightly parted.
Scattered petals on the counter surface and floor from @Ash's hand slam.
Camera: static, same position. @Luna in foreground left, closed door background center.
Lighting: dim daylight bars through blinds only. No more bright doorway light.

Sound: quiet sobbing, hand extending through air, footstep backward on tile,
slow heavy footsteps toward door, door handle turning, pause.
[Ash, quiet broken male voice, facing the door, not turning back]:
"Don't come find me."
Door opens. Rush of outside air. Door closes heavily. Echo. Silence.
```

## 클립 3: 결심 (10초)

**키프레임**: `ep09_s5_end.png`
**Scene Reference**: L1_flower_shop_dark
**Element**: Luna

```
Close-up, camera positioned at counter level, shooting straight at @Luna's face.
@Luna stands behind the counter center frame. Thin daylight bars through blinds cross @Luna's face horizontally.

0-4 seconds: @Luna stands motionless. @Luna's eyes fixed on the closed door where @Ash left.
@Luna's face still wet. @Luna's mouth corners pulled down, brow crumpled, chin trembling once.
@Luna's right hand rests on the counter. @Luna's left hand hangs at her side.
Half-arranged flowers on the counter beside @Luna's right hand. Scattered petals.
@Luna exhales slowly through slightly open mouth. @Luna's shoulders drop.
Camera: static close-up on @Luna's face.
Lighting: thin daylight bars through blinds across @Luna's face. Dim amber lamp glow from counter.

4-7 seconds: A phone on the counter buzzes. Screen lights up with a name.
@Luna's eyes move down to the phone screen. @Luna does not pick it up immediately.
Phone buzzes again. Screen glow reflects on @Luna's chin and jaw.
@Luna's right hand reaches slowly and picks up the phone. Buzz stops.
@Luna holds the phone at chest level, screen facing her. @Luna stares at the screen.
Camera: slow push-in from close-up to extreme close-up on @Luna's eyes and the phone glow.
Lighting: phone screen glow on @Luna's lower face. Daylight bars on forehead and eyes.

7-10 seconds: @Luna's expression shifts: brow lowers, crumpled lines smooth out.
@Luna's eyes narrow. @Luna's jaw clenches. @Luna's lips press into a thin hard line.
@Luna's grip tightens on the phone. @Luna's knuckles whiten.
@Luna looks up from the phone, eyes fixed forward toward the closed door.
@Luna's face in the daylight bars: eyes steady and unblinking, lips flat, jaw set.
Camera: holds extreme close-up on @Luna's eyes. Daylight bars across her face like prison bars.
Lighting: thin daylight bars through blinds. Dim amber from counter lamp. Phone screen glow fading.

Sound: phone buzzing twice on counter surface, @Luna's hand picking up phone, buzz stops.
Silence. Only @Luna's breathing, slow and controlled.
No dialogue.
```
