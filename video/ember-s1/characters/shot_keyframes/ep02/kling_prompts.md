**Scene Reference**: L3_rainy_street
**Element**: Luna

```

Rain falls heavy. Neon signs on both sides cast blue and purple reflections in puddles on asphalt.
Single orange streetlight at background center.

0-5 seconds: @Luna appears at background center in dark coat, walking toward camera.
@Luna's body facing camera, head tilted slightly down, eyes fixed on the ground ahead.
@Luna's lips pressed together in a thin hard line, jaw clenched, brow lowered.
@Luna's left hand grips coat collar closed at her neck. Right hand hangs at her side, fist clenched.

5-9 seconds: @Luna reaches foreground left of frame. Camera cuts to medium shot.
@Luna stops in front of a heavy black door, right side of frame.
@Luna's right hand reaches for the door handle. @Luna's left hand releases coat collar.
@Luna turns head left, looks back over left shoulder down the empty street.
@Luna's eyes narrow, nostrils flare slightly, lips part then press shut again.
Camera: static medium shot from @Luna's right side at 45 degrees.

9-12 seconds: @Luna pushes door open with right hand. Cold blue neon light floods out from inside.
@Luna steps forward through the doorway, body passing from orange streetlight into blue neon.
@Luna's face half-lit blue from the interior, half-lit orange from behind.
@Luna's silhouette shrinks into the blue-lit interior. Door swings shut behind her.
Camera: static, shooting from street into doorway.

Lighting: orange streetlight from above and behind. Cold blue neon spilling from club door.
Rain reflects both colors on wet pavement.

Sound: heavy rain on pavement, distant traffic, footsteps splashing in puddles,
door handle turning, heavy door creaking open, bass thump from inside,
door slamming shut, rain muffled.
No dialogue.
```

## 클립 2: 더치스의 거래 (15초) ⭐ VIRAL CLIP

**키프레임**: `ep02_clip2_duchess_deal.png`
**Scene Reference**: L2_duchess_club
**Element**: Luna + Duchess

```
Low-angle medium shot, camera positioned below sofa level, shooting upward.
Dark leather sofa center frame. Cold blue neon strip along the wall behind.
Deep shadows across the ceiling. Dark glass table in foreground.

0-4 seconds: @Duchess seated on dark leather sofa center frame, body leaning back, legs crossed.
@Duchess's left arm rests along sofa back. Right hand holds a wine glass at chest height.
@Duchess's ice blue eyes fixed straight ahead, chin tilted slightly down.
@Duchess's lips closed, one corner of mouth raised slightly, eyes narrowed. Control.
Blue neon reflects off @Duchess's pale skin and jewelry at neck and wrists.
Camera: static low-angle. @Duchess dominates the frame.

Sound: ice shifting in glass, quiet club bass pulse through walls.
[Duchess, calm low female voice, chin tilted down, eyes fixed forward]: "Sit down."

4-9 seconds: Camera cuts to over-the-shoulder shot from behind @Duchess's right shoulder.
@Luna visible sitting in a chair opposite, left side of frame.
@Luna's body stiff, back straight, hands on her own knees, fingers gripping fabric.
@Luna's face lit by cold blue neon from above. Auburn hair frames her cheekbones.
@Luna's eyes look directly at @Duchess's face. @Luna's jaw tight, lips pressed flat.
@Duchess's right hand sets wine glass on table. @Duchess leans forward 10cm toward @Luna.
@Duchess's left hand gestures palm-down slowly toward @Luna.
Camera: slow push-in from medium toward @Luna's face over @Duchess's shoulder.

Sound: wine glass set on table, leather creaking as @Duchess leans forward.
[Duchess, smooth controlled female voice, leaning toward @Luna]: "I'll keep your brother safe. No one touches him."
[Duchess, same voice, left hand palm-down]: "You work for me. Small things. Nothing you can't handle."

9-15 seconds: Close-up two-shot, both faces in profile from left side.
@Luna left of frame facing right. @Duchess right of frame facing left. 50cm apart.
@Luna's eyes drop from @Duchess's face down to her own lap.
@Luna's throat moves as she swallows. @Luna's brow creases, lips tremble once then press shut.
@Luna closes eyes for two seconds. @Luna opens eyes, looks back at @Duchess.
@Luna nods once, slow. @Luna's eyes half-lidded, brow still creased.
@Duchess's eyes track @Luna's face without blinking. @Duchess's lips curve into a slow controlled smile.
@Duchess's chin lifts slightly, eyes narrow with satisfaction.
Camera: static close-up two-shot in profile from left side.

Lighting: cold blue neon strip behind sofa. Small amber spot on table surface.
Deep shadow between the two faces.

Sound: @Luna swallowing, fabric shifting, @Duchess exhaling slowly through nose.
Long silence after the nod. Low frequency hum.
No dialogue in final section.
```
