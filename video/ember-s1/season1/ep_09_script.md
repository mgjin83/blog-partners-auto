## 씬 구성

### S1 — HOOK (0:00~0:05)

**연출:** 꽃집. 낮. 블라인드가 닫혀있다. 빛이 틈새로만 들어온다. 줄무늬 빛. 루나가 꽃을 정리하고 있다. 문이 거칠게 열린다. 빛이 쏟아진다. 애쉬의 실루엣.

**대사:** 없음

**Kling 프롬프트:**
```
Medium shot of a flower shop interior with closed blinds,
thin strips of daylight cutting through the slats onto flowers,
++a young woman with auburn hair++ arranging flowers in the striped light,
the door bursting open with bright daylight flooding in,
a young man's silhouette in the doorway,
camera holds static, harsh daylight through opening door
contrasting with the dim striped interior, intrusion and rupture,
cinematic, shallow depth of field, film noir aesthetic,
desaturated color palette, high contrast, low-key lighting,
anamorphic lens, professional color grading
```

---

### S2 — 애쉬의 분노 (0:05~0:18) [VIRAL_CLIP]

**연출:** 애쉬가 안으로 들어온다. 표정 — 분노와 절망이 섞여있다. 루나를 향해 온다. 루나가 꽃을 내려놓는다. 애쉬가 말한다. 루나가 입을 연다. 가장 조용하고 가장 잔인한 진실.

**대사:**
- ASH: *(분노)* "I know everything. What you've been doing."
- LUNA: *(조용히)* "..."
- ASH: "I never asked you to do that!"
- LUNA: *(올려다보며)* "You did the same thing."
- ASH: *(흔들리며)* "That's different!"
- LUNA: "How?"
- *(긴 침묵)*

**Kling 프롬프트:**
```
Medium two-shot of ++a young man with dark brown hair++ confronting
++a young woman with auburn hair++ in a dim flower shop,
Ash's face showing rage mixed with despair,
Luna looking up with calm exhaustion in her green eyes,
flowers between them on the counter,
camera slow push-in on both faces as tension peaks,
thin strips of daylight through blinds creating bars of light
across both figures, confrontation and mirror,
cinematic, shallow depth of field, film noir aesthetic,
desaturated color palette, high contrast, low-key lighting,
anamorphic lens, professional color grading
```

---

### S3 — 침묵 (0:18~0:25)

**연출:** 침묵이 흐른다. 애쉬가 답을 못한다. 루나의 말이 맞기 때문이다. 둘 다 같은 짓을 했다 — 상대를 위해 자신을 더럽혔다. 애쉬의 눈에 눈물이 고인다. 분노가 아니라 슬픔으로 바뀐다. 루나가 손을 뻗으려 한다. 애쉬가 물러난다.

**대사:** 없음

**Kling 프롬프트:**
```
Close-up of ++a young man with dark brown hair++ with tears forming,
rage dissolving into grief in hazel eyes,
then cut to ++Luna's hand++ reaching out slowly,
Ash stepping back away from the outstretched hand,
dim flower shop with light bars from blinds,
camera holds static, thin daylight bars across Ash's face
like prison bars, the space between them widening,
broken symmetry,
cinematic, shallow depth of field, film noir aesthetic,
desaturated color palette, high contrast, low-key lighting,
anamorphic lens, professional color grading
```

---

### S4 — 애쉬의 퇴장 (0:25~0:35)

**연출:** 애쉬가 돌아선다. 문 쪽으로 간다. 말한다. 문을 열고 나간다. 밝은 바깥. 문이 닫힌다. 꽃집이 다시 어두워진다. 루나가 서 있다. 혼자. 바닥에 꽃잎 몇 개가 떨어져 있다 — 애쉬가 들어올 때 건드린 것.

**대사:**
- ASH: *(문 앞에서, 돌아보지 않고)* "Don't come find me."

**Kling 프롬프트:**
```
Medium shot of ++a young man with dark brown hair++ walking to the door,
back to camera, pausing at the threshold,
then pushing through into bright daylight outside,
the door closing and the flower shop falling back into dim striped light,
scattered petals on the floor from the disrupted display,
camera holds static, bright exterior through doorway then darkness
as door closes, departure and finality,
cinematic, shallow depth of field, film noir aesthetic,
desaturated color palette, high contrast, low-key lighting,
anamorphic lens, professional color grading
```

---

### S5 — UNRESOLVED END (0:35~0:45)

**연출:** 빈 꽃집. 루나 혼자. 서 있다. 움직이지 않는다. 카운터 위에 꽃이 정리되다 만 채로 있다. 폰이 울린다. 루나가 폰을 본다. 화면에 "DUCHESS" 이름이 빛난다. 루나가 폰을 집어든다. 받지는 않는다. 폰을 쥔 채 서 있다. 눈이 달라진다 — 분노도 슬픔도 아닌, 무언가를 결심한 눈.

**대사:** 없음

**Kling 프롬프트:**
```
Close-up of ++a young woman with auburn hair++ standing motionless
in a dim flower shop, a phone buzzing on the counter,
the screen glowing with a name, Luna picking it up
but not answering, green eyes shifting from grief
to something harder and more deliberate,
half-arranged flowers on the counter beside her,
camera slow push-in on Luna's eyes,
thin daylight bars from blinds casting lines across her face,
decision forming in darkness,
cinematic, shallow depth of field, film noir aesthetic,
desaturated color palette, high contrast, low-key lighting,
anamorphic lens, professional color grading
```
