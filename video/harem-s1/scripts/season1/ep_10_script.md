# EP10: BEGINNING

**Duration:** 50s | **Scenes:** 13 | **Dialogue:** 1
**HOOK TEXT:** "Aunt's back. Game over... or is it?"

---

### S01 | 0-2s | THUMB-STOP
> Medium, camera from inside hallway, facing front door

Front door opens. Diana steps in — business casual, roller suitcase, tote bag on shoulder. She pauses in the doorway, scanning the hallway. In the background through the archway to the living room: five figures standing in a row. Ethan center. Tasha and Mika flanking him. Serena and Lily behind. All wearing identical tight smiles. Rigid postures. Hands clasped in front of them like a police lineup.

---

### S02 | 2-6s
> Medium, camera from living room, facing the group + Diana

Diana walks in. Drops her tote. She looks at the five of them — her eyes narrow for one second, reading the room. The tension is thick enough to see. She doesn't address it. She turns toward the kitchen, heels clicking.

---

### S03 | 6-10s
> CU, camera from inside refrigerator (fun angle), shooting outward

Diana opens the fridge door. Camera from inside the fridge: Diana's face fills the frame. Her eyes scan the shelves — fully stocked. Organized containers labeled by day of the week. Fresh fruit in bowls. Prepared meals in clear containers. All Tasha's handiwork. Diana's eyebrows rise. A slow, genuine smile spreads.

---

### --- TRANSITION 1: 귀국 긴장 → 해소 ---

---

### S04 | 10-14s
> Medium, camera from kitchen island

Diana closes the fridge. Looks around the house — clean floors, organized bookshelves, fresh flowers on the dining table, no dust. She walks back to the living room. Her face has softened — the business mask replaced by something maternal. She walks to Ethan. Reaches up and ruffles his hair with one hand — firm, warm, the way a parent does.

---

### S05 | 14-18s
> CU, Diana and Ethan

Diana cups Ethan's face in both hands. Looks at him — really examines. She sees something she didn't expect. When he arrived, his eyes were hollow. Now they're alive. His cheeks have color. His posture is different — not hunched and guarded, but upright. His shoulders are back. She nods once — slow, certain. Her eyes glisten for a moment. She lets go, pats his cheek.

---

### S06 | 18-23s
> Medium-Wide, camera from the corner of the room

Everyone sits. Diana in the armchair, legs crossed, coffee in hand. The five on the sofa and floor — their usual positions but conscious, no automatic cuddling. Silence. Diana looks at each girl's face in sequence — at how each one keeps glancing at Ethan when they think no one's watching. A tiny smirk crosses Diana's lips. She looks at Ethan — at how he's not flinching away from any of them anymore. He's sitting still, calm, present. She hides the smirk behind her coffee mug.

---

### --- TRANSITION 2: 해소 → Ethan의 변화 (첫 자연스러운 미소) ---

---

### S07 | 23-28s
> CU, Ethan, then Medium pulling outward

Ethan looks around the room. His gaze settles on each person:
- Tasha: gives him a soft, tentative smile — lips together, eyes warm, head slightly tilted
- Mika: meets his eyes for one beat, cheeks flush pink, then looks at her own hands in her lap
- Serena: nods once — small, precise, sincere, her dark eyes steady
- Lily: gives a small wave with just her fingers, biting her lower lip, dimples appearing

Ethan's face transforms. The wall is gone. No red cheeks, no panic, no flinching, no looking away. For the first time in ten episodes: a genuine, natural, unreserved smile. The corners of his mouth lift. His eyes crinkle at the corners. Warmth radiates from his expression. It's the smile from his mother's photo — he has her smile.

---

### S08 | 28-31s
> Rapid CU cuts — each girl's reaction (1s each)

Tasha: her hand goes to her chest, fingers spread over her heart. Her eyes brighten — shining with moisture, but it's joy.
Mika: startled — eyes widen. Then a slow matching smile builds on her face, showing teeth for the first time in two episodes.
Serena: her composure breaks — eyes go wide, then she looks down, and a real blush spreads across her cheekbones. First genuine blush of the series.
Lily: both hands clap over her mouth. Her hazel eyes shine. Her dimples deep as canyons.

---

### --- TRANSITION 3: Diana 재출장 → 전원 합류 ---

---

### S09 | 31-35s
> Medium, Diana in armchair, camera from the side

Diana's phone buzzes. She answers, listens, her expression shifting — jaw setting, business face returning. She stands up, straightening her blouse. Walks toward the entrance. Grabs her suitcase handle, pops it up. She turns back to the room.

---

### S10 | 35-38s
> Medium, camera from hallway, Diana in the doorway

Diana in the doorway, one foot over the threshold. She looks at Ethan. Looks at the four girls. Her expression: apologetic but trusting.

**DIALOGUE:** "Take care of him." (Diana, 4 words, warm, with a wink)

She steps out. The door clicks shut.

---

### S11 | 38-42s
> Medium-Wide, camera from the corner, showing all five

The door-click echoes. Five people in the living room. The air changes — the authority figure is gone again. Silence hangs for two beats. The four girls look at each other. It's not a competition anymore. A shared look passes between them — Tasha to Mika, Mika to Serena, Serena to Lily. Something is agreed without words. All four turn toward Ethan simultaneously.

---

### S12 | 42-47s
> Medium, camera from in front of Ethan

Four girls moving toward him. Tasha from the left, reaching out. Mika from the right, stepping forward. Serena rising from the sofa behind. Lily popping up from the floor. Ethan sees them coming. All four. At once.

Old Ethan would have run. Old Ethan would have covered his eyes. Old Ethan would have locked himself in the bathroom.

New Ethan takes a breath. Smiles — wider this time, the smile reaching his eyes, crinkling them. He opens both arms. Wide. Palms up. An invitation. A surrender. An acceptance.

---

### S13 | 47-50s | CLIFFHANGER
> Top-down camera (crane/drone angle), pulling up → Cut to insert CU

All five together. A tangle of arms. Tasha's head on his left shoulder, her platinum hair bright. Mika's head on his right, ponytail draping over his arm. Serena's arms around all of them from behind, dark hair falling forward. Lily squeezed into the center at his chest, face beaming up at him. Ethan at the center — eyes closed, that smile still there, chin tilted up.

Cut to: the staircase wall. A row of framed photos. Tasha's school portrait. Mika's team photo. Diana's professional headshot. A family photo of the three of them.

And next to it — a new frame. Slightly crooked, recently hung. Ethan's photo. A candid shot — him half-smiling, looking off-camera, caught in a moment of unguarded warmth. Someone cared enough to print it, frame it, and hang it on this wall.

Hold for two beats. String lights outside the window cast a warm glow.

CUT TO BLACK.
