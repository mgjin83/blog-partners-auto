# EP08: HEART

**Duration:** 50s | **Scenes:** 12 | **Dialogue:** 0
**HOOK TEXT:** "For the first time, he reached out."

---

### S01 | 0-3s | THUMB-STOP
> CU, camera at lounger level, shooting slightly upward, night

Night. Poolside. String lights above casting warm dots. Pool glowing aquamarine behind. Ethan alone on a lounger, knees drawn up. Phone in both hands, screen illuminating his face from below. On the screen: a photo — a woman with warm eyes and his same nose, holding a baby. His mom. His thumb rests on the screen, unmoving. His expression: mouth set in a hard line, brow pulled together, eyes glistening with moisture that hasn't fallen yet.

---

### S02 | 3-7s
> CU, phone screen (insert) → CU Ethan's face

Insert: Mom's photo fills the frame. She's smiling — laugh lines, genuine joy. She's young. The baby in her arms is reaching for her face.
Cut to: Ethan. He lifts the phone to his chest, screen pressed flat against his sternum, over his heart. His eyes close. His lower lip trembles once — a single involuntary spasm. He tilts his head back. His throat works around a swallow that takes too long.

---

### S03 | 7-11s
> Extreme CU, Ethan's closed eyes, camera directly above face

A tear escapes from the corner of his right eye. It tracks down his temple, curves past his ear. His jaw muscle flexes under the skin — once, twice. He breathes in through his nose — shaky, uneven, the breath catching twice. He exhales through his mouth — longer, slower, but still trembling.

### --- TRANSITION 1: 외로움 → 위로의 도착 ---

---

### S04 | 11-15s
> Medium, camera from the sliding glass door, shooting outward

The glass door slides open. Tasha steps out. Barefoot. Wearing a soft oversized cream-colored sweater that reaches past mid-thigh. No makeup, hair loose and natural. She carries a folded grey blanket against her chest. She sees Ethan — his posture, the phone on his chest, the wet track on his temple. Her playful expression from every other episode vanishes. What replaces it: quiet understanding, no performance.

---

### S05 | 15-19s
> Medium, camera from behind Ethan's lounger

Tasha reaches him. She unfolds the blanket. Drapes it gently over his shoulders, tucking the edges under his arms without pulling. She sits on the edge of the lounger next to his hip, facing the pool. She doesn't touch him. Doesn't hug him. Doesn't fill the silence. She just sits. Hands in her lap. Present.

---

### S06 | 19-24s
> CU, both faces in profile, camera from the side, pool light reflecting

Pool light ripples across their faces in shifting blue-green patterns. Thirty seconds pass in story-time silence. Tasha reaches over — her fingers gently brush the hair from Ethan's forehead. Her fingertips trail down his temple, along his jaw. Light as air. He doesn't flinch. He doesn't pull away. For the first time in 7 episodes, he allows someone's touch without a single muscle tensing. A new tear falls from his left eye.

---

### S07 | 24-27s
> CU, Tasha's hand on Ethan's face

Tasha's thumb catches the tear on his cheek. Wipes it, slow. Her fingers rest against his jaw. Her expression: no smile, no sadness — just steady warmth. Eyes entirely on him, reflecting the pool light. Her thumb strokes once more across the damp skin.

---

### --- TRANSITION 2: 위로 → 선 넘기 (Ethan의 첫 능동적 접촉) ---

---

### S08 | 27-32s
> CU, Ethan's hand on lounger armrest, camera at armrest level

Ethan's right hand on the lounger armrest. His fist uncurls — slowly, finger by finger, like a decision being made in real time. His hand slides across the fabric toward Tasha's hand resting on the lounger edge. His fingers reach hers. Touch. Wrap around. Squeeze. His knuckles go white with the force of his grip. He doesn't look at her. Head still tilted back. But his hand says everything.

---

### S09 | 29-34s
> CU, their clasped hands from directly above

Two hands interlocked. His fingers between hers. His grip tight — desperate — the grip of someone who hasn't held anyone's hand in years. Her thumb moves — strokes over his knuckle, slow, steady. Pool light plays across their skin — blue, then green, then blue. This is the first time Ethan has reached for anyone since his mother died.

---

### S10 | 34-39s
> Medium, both figures, camera from the pool side, wide enough to see the setting

Tasha shifts. She leans her head down onto Ethan's shoulder. Her platinum hair spills over his arm and the blanket. His head tilts — rests on top of hers. Brown hair on gold. Two people, one blanket, legs stretched out on the lounger. String lights blurred into warm bokeh behind them. The pool surface still, reflecting both their shapes.

### --- TRANSITION 3: 달달 정점 → Mika 목격 (상처) ---

### S11 | 39-44s
> Medium, camera from inside the house, shooting outward through the glass door

Inside the dark house, shooting through the sliding glass door. Mika stands at the glass — she came downstairs. She can see everything: Ethan and Tasha by the pool, hands clasped, heads together, string lights above them. The glass reflects Mika's face faintly — ghostly double exposure over the scene outside. Her right hand is on the door handle. Her grip tightens. Knuckles go white — matching Ethan's from minutes ago, but for a different reason.

---

### S12 | 44-50s | CLIFFHANGER
> CU, Mika's face from inside, shot through the glass (pool scene reflected behind her)

Mika's face. Lips pressed together so hard they've gone bloodless. Jaw trembling — a vibration in her chin she can't control. Her eyes are glassy — full of moisture, surface tension holding. One blink would break it. She doesn't blink. She takes one sharp breath through her nose — her nostrils flare. Her eyes squeeze shut — tears pressed out at the corners but she catches them with the heel of her hand, angry at herself. She turns away from the glass. Her hand releases the door handle. She walks into the darkness of the hallway. Her silhouette swallowed by shadow.

CUT TO BLACK.
