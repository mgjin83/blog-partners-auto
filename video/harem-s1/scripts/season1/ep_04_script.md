Her chest pressed against his back — her breasts flattened against his shoulder blades. Her hair cascading over his right shoulder, black strands on his grey t-shirt. Her lips beside his left ear — close enough that her breath moves the fine hair above his ear.

### S02 | 3-6s
> CU, Ethan's face from front, camera at eye level

Ethan's eyes on the textbook but glazed — not reading a single word. His jaw clenched so hard the masseter muscles are visible. Adam's apple bobs with a tight swallow. His right ear — the one closest to Serena's mouth — is bright red, almost glowing.

---

### S03 | 6-10s
> Medium, camera from side of table

Serena shifts. Leans in further. Her left hand lifts off the book and lands on Ethan's forearm. Her thumb begins drawing slow, small circles on his bare skin. Her right hand points at a line in the textbook — leaning her chin down onto his shoulder to "see better." The side of her face against the side of his. Ethan stops breathing. His chest goes completely still. His pen drops from his fingers, rolls off the table.

Lily on her back. Her right leg extended straight up. She grabs Ethan's wrist and pulls his hand to her ankle. His other hand she places on her knee. ### S08 | 23-27s
> Medium, camera inside bathroom facing mirror

Ethan bursts in. Slams the door. Grabs the sink with both hands — arms locked straight, head hanging between his shoulders. He stares at his reflection: face red, hair messy, eyes wild, shirt collar stretched from Lily pulling him. He turns the cold faucet. Cups water. Splashes it onto his face. Water runs down his jaw, drips onto his shirt. Mouth open, breathing hard.

### S09 | 27-30s

Ethan straightens. Wipes his face with his shirt hem, exposing a flash of stomach. Takes one long breath through his nose, exhales through his mouth. Sets his jaw. Reaches for the door handle. Turns it.

### S10 | 30-33s
> Medium, camera from inside bathroom looking outward

Door opens. Ethan steps into the hallway. He stops.

---

### S11 | 33-37s
> Medium-CU, camera from behind Ethan's shoulder

Mika is directly in front of him. She was leaning against the opposite wall — arms crossed, one foot flat against the wall behind her. She saw everything — Serena's tutoring, Lily's stretching. She pushes off the wall with her shoulder. Her arms uncross. She takes one step forward. Her green eyes locked on his. No casual grin. Something entirely different.

---

### S12 | 37-42s
> Medium, camera from hallway side, showing both in profile

Ethan steps back. His back hits the bathroom doorframe. Mika follows — one more step. The hallway is narrow — three feet wall to wall. She's close. Closer than tutoring-Serena. Closer than stretching-Lily. Her expression: jaw set, brow slightly lowered, lips slightly parted. This is not playful Mika.

---

### S13 | 42-46s
> CU, camera from below, angled up at both faces

Mika raises her right hand. Palm flat, she places it on the wall beside Ethan's head — fingers spread against the drywall. Kabedon. She leans in. Her face tilts five degrees right. Her green eyes travel from his brown eyes down to his lips — they linger there — then climb back up. Her lips part another millimeter. A strand of honey-brown hair falls across her cheekbone.

---

### S14 | 46-50s | CLIFFHANGER
> Extreme CU, split composition — her face left, his face right, in profile

Two faces. Four inches apart. Mika's green iris fills the left side — unblinking, searching, fierce. Ethan's brown iris fills the right — wide, frozen, but for the first time not looking away. Not running. The hallway light casts a sharp diagonal shadow between them. Mika's lips part. The gap between them hums.
