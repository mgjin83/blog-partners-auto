### S01 | 0-3s | THUMB-STOP
> POV (Ethan's eyes opening), camera blur-to-focus

Black. Blur. Focus. First thing in view: Tasha's sleeping face, three inches away. Her platinum hair fanned across Ethan's pillow and arm. Her right leg draped across his hip. Her left arm across his chest, fingers curled into his shirt. Camisole strap slipped off her left shoulder — fabric sagging, revealing the slope of her collarbone and beyond. Her slow breath hits his neck.

---

### S02 | 3-6s
> CU, Ethan's face from above

Ethan's brain boots up. His eyes register what they're seeing. Pupils dilate. Mouth falls open. He looks down — her leg across him. Looks at her face — inches away. Looks at the slipped strap. Color drains from his face, then floods back — crimson wave rising from neck to forehead.

---

### S03 | 6-10s
> Medium, camera from bedside at mattress level

Ethan attempts escape. He slides one inch right. Tasha mumbles, brow furrows in sleep, her arm tightens across his chest. Her leg hooks tighter around his hip, pulling him back. He freezes. Second attempt — lifts her arm gently by the wrist. She rolls toward him — face buries into his neck, nose and lips against his skin. Her body now flush against his side. Ethan's face: pure silent scream.

---

### S04 | 10-14s
> Wide, camera from room corner, shooting diagonally

Final attempt. Ethan peels himself sideways in one desperate motion. He rolls off the edge. Falls — hits the floor with a thud. Sits up on the carpet, back against the bed frame, chest heaving. One hand pressed to his heart, feeling it hammer. On the bed above him: Tasha sprawled across where he was, one arm hanging over the edge, still asleep. Peaceful face. Camisole twisted.

### S07 | 21-24s
> CU, Ethan in doorway, camera at his eye level

Ethan's reaction: eyes blow wide. Both hands slap over his face — fingers pressed tight together. He stumbles backward — one step, two steps. His back hits the hallway wall. He slides down to sitting position on the floor. Hands still clamped over eyes. Visible through his fingers: scarlet cheeks, scarlet ears, scarlet neck.
