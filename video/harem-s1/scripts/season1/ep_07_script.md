# EP07: POOL

**Duration:** 50s | **Scenes:** 14 | **Dialogue:** 1
**HOOK TEXT:** "4 bikinis. 1 pool. I'm dead."

### S01 | 0-3s | THUMB-STOP
> CU, camera at Tasha's lower-back level, angled up

Tasha standing at the pool edge, back to camera. White bikini — triangle top, string ties. She looks over her right shoulder at Ethan. Her left hand holds the untied bikini top strings behind her neck. Her right hand presses the front cups against her chest. She tilts her head toward her exposed back — come here, tie this.

---

### S02 | 3-7s
> Medium-Wide, camera from pool edge, full scene establishing

Backyard. Pool sparkling under California sun. Four girls stand in a line on the pool deck: Tasha (white triangle bikini, full curves on display), Mika (black sport bikini, cut high on hips, abs carved), Serena (red string bikini, minimal fabric, confident posture), Lily (pink ruffled bikini, cute but the ruffles don't hide much). Ethan in navy swim trunks and a white t-shirt — the shirt is his last line of defense. He stands three feet back, slightly behind a lounge chair, as if it's a shield.

---

### S03 | 7-10s
> CU, Ethan's face, camera at his eye level, rapid cuts simulated

Ethan trying to find somewhere safe to look. Eyes dart left — Mika bending forward touching her toes, hamstring stretch. Eyes dart right — Serena adjusting her bikini strap, pulling it with one finger, letting it snap back. Eyes forward — Lily waving both hands, bouncing on her toes, everything bouncing. Eyes up — sky. He keeps them there. His jaw is clenched so tight his teeth might crack.

### S04 | 10-14s
> Medium, camera from poolside, shooting toward lounger

Serena lies face-down on a lounger. She holds up a bottle of sunscreen toward Ethan without looking — arm extended backward. Her other hand reaches behind her back and pulls her bikini top tie loose. The strings fall to the sides. Her bare back — shoulder blades, spine, the dimples above her bikini bottom — exposed. She turns her head on the towel to look at Ethan. Smiles.

### S05 | 14-17s
> CU, Ethan's hands, camera from above

Ethan's hands. Sunscreen squeezed onto his right palm — too much, it overflows down his wrist. His hand hovers one inch above Serena's shoulder blade. Trembling. He can see the individual goosebumps on her skin. His fingers make contact — barely — just the fingertips pressing on her shoulder. His other hand grabs his own wrist to steady the shaking.

### S06 | 17-20s
> Medium, camera at water surface level from inside the pool

SPLASH — Lily cannonballs in right next to Ethan who is sitting on the pool edge with his legs in the water. A wall of water engulfs him. When the spray clears: Lily surfaced directly between his knees, looking up at him, hair slicked back, water streaming down her face, grinning. Both her hands on his knees, propping herself up.

### S07 | 20-24s
> Medium, camera from poolside corner

Pool fight. Mika and Ethan in the water, chest-deep. Mika splashes. Ethan blocks with his arm, turns away. Mika lunges — grabs his shoulders, pushes him under. Underwater tumble. They surface together — gasping. Mika's legs are wrapped around Ethan's waist, locked at the ankles. Her arms around his neck. Face to face, noses six inches apart. Water streaming between them.

---

### S08 | 23-26s
> CU, Mika and Ethan, camera at water level

Mika's face inches from Ethan's. Water droplets on her eyelashes, on her upper lip. Her green eyes dancing — the old playful Mika for this moment. Her legs still locked around him under the surface. She doesn't untangle. He can feel her legs tightening.

**DIALOGUE:** "Oops." (Mika, 1 word, grinning, not letting go)

Ethan plants both hands on her shoulders and pushes backward through the water, breaking free. He swims toward the pool edge.

### S09 | 26-30s
> Medium, camera from above pool edge, shooting down

Ethan at the pool edge. Both hands on the concrete lip, pulling himself up. Biceps flexing, water streaming down. He gets one knee up on the deck. His foot slips on the wet tile — his knee slides.

### S10 | 30-34s
> Medium-CU, camera from pool deck, low angle

Ethan falls forward. Tasha was standing right there, reaching for a towel. He crashes into her. They go down onto the pool lounger. His hands catch on either side of her head — arms locked, holding himself above her. Their bodies aligned, his above hers. His dripping chest over her bikini-clad body. A water droplet falls from the tip of his nose — lands on her cheek.

---

### S11 | 34-38s
> Extreme CU, two faces, camera from the side

Tasha and Ethan. Noses almost touching. Water dripping from his hair onto her face. Her blue eyes widen — then soften. They drop to his lips. Stay there. Her own lips part, lower lip glistening with pool water. Ethan's eyes locked on hers — wide, frozen, but something else is there now. Two seconds of absolute stillness. Only dripping water.

---

### S12 | 38-42s
> Medium, camera from pool deck, side angle

Ethan springs off her. Pushes himself up like the lounger is electrified. Stumbles backward two steps. Snatches a towel from a chair. Presses it to his face — covering everything from forehead to chin. Stands there. Chest heaving. Towel clamped to his face with both hands.

---

### S13 | 42-46s
> Medium, camera from behind Ethan

Ethan slowly lowers the towel. His breathing steadies. He lifts his head. His eyes focus on one of the four girls. His expression shifts — not the usual panic. Not red-cheeked terror. Something softer. His brow relaxes. His lips part slightly. His eyes are steady, unblinking. He's seeing this person — really seeing them — for the first time.

---

### S14 | 46-50s | CLIFFHANGER
> Extreme CU, Ethan's right eye, macro lens

Ethan's eye fills the frame. The iris — deep brown with gold flecks in the California sunlight. In the curved reflection of his cornea: a girl's silhouette. Backlit by the sun, shape unclear. Could be anyone. His pupil dilates — slow, unmistakable, involuntary.
