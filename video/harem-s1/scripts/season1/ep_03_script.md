# EP03: VISITORS

**Duration:** 50s | **Scenes:** 14 | **Dialogue:** 1
**HOOK TEXT:** "Her friends came over. I need to leave this house."

---

### S01 | 0-2s | THUMB-STOP
> Medium, camera from inside hallway, shooting toward front door

Front door opens. Serena leans against the doorframe — tight black dress ending above mid-thigh, strappy heels, gold necklace resting on her collarbone. Hair flowing free, glossy black. She locks eyes with Ethan who opened the door. Her gaze travels from his sneakers, up his jeans, past his t-shirt, to his face. One corner of her mouth lifts. Slow.

---

### S02 | 2-5s
> CU, their hands + faces, camera at waist level then up

Serena extends her right hand. Ethan takes it — standard handshake. She doesn't release. Her thumb slides slowly across the back of his hand, tracing a line over his knuckles. She pulls gently — he stumbles forward half a step. Their faces now twelve inches apart. She holds eye contact, unblinking. He doesn't.

---

### S03 | 5-9s
> Medium, camera from the side of the doorway

Lily bursts in from behind Serena — short, bouncing on her toes, copper-brown bob swinging. She spots Ethan, eyes go wide (pupils visibly dilate), mouth opens into a grin with deep dimples. She grabs his right arm with both of hers and squeezes — his forearm pressed flat between her chest, her arms clamped tight. She bounces in place, pulling his arm with her. Ethan's right side goes rigid, his free left hand rises in a helpless wave.

### S04 | 9-13s

Living room. Serena sits first — crosses her right leg over left toward where Ethan will sit, skirt riding up, thigh exposed to hip. She pats the cushion beside her. Ethan forced to sit by Lily pushing his back. He drops onto the sofa. Lily takes his other side — tucks her legs under her, leans her entire upper body into his arm. Serena's crossed knee now pressing against Ethan's thigh.

### S05 | 13-16s

Ethan gripping sofa cushion with both hands — knuckles bloodless. Eyes fixed forward on the blank TV screen. Jaw locked. Left side of frame: Serena's crossed bare leg. Right side: Lily's head on his shoulder, her hair against his neck. He's not breathing — visible freeze.

The back of Ethan's skull presses into her chest. She smiles down at Serena — bright smile, but her jaw is tight. Eyes sharp.

### S09 | 26-29s
> Medium, camera from the entrance

Ethan stands up abruptly — four sets of arms and legs slide off him. He snatches a water glass from the coffee table. Walks fast toward the kitchen — long strides, stiff legs. Behind him: four girls on the sofa, all eyes tracking him.

### S10 | 29-33s

Kitchen. Ethan drinks water — the glass shakes against his teeth. He holds the counter edge with his other hand. In the background through the open floor plan: the four girls on the sofa. Serena whispers something to Tasha, chin tilted. Lily points toward Ethan. Mika watches silently, arms crossed.
