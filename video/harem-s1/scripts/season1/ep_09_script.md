# EP09: STORM

**Duration:** 50s | **Scenes:** 13 | **Dialogue:** 1
**HOOK TEXT:** "They were all serious."

---

### S01 | 0-2s | THUMB-STOP
> Medium, camera from inside Ethan's room, facing the door

Ethan's bedroom door slams open — bang against the wall. Mika fills the doorframe. Tank top and shorts, barefoot. Her eyes are red-rimmed, swollen — she's been crying. Not recently, but enough for the evidence to stay. Her usual bright energy is gone. She stands there, chest heaving, fists at her sides.

---

### S02 | 2-5s
> CU, Ethan, camera from bed level looking up

Ethan was lying in bed, phone in hand. He bolts upright at the slam. His eyes go directly to Mika's face — her red eyes. His expression shifts from startled to something he hasn't shown before: concern. His brow furrows. His mouth parts. He swings his legs off the bed, phone dropped on the sheets.

---

### S03 | 5-9s
> Medium, camera from beside the bed

Mika walks in. Three strides. She grabs Ethan's right wrist — her fingers wrap tight around his bones. She pulls him to standing in one motion. They face each other. She's slightly shorter in bare feet — she tilts her chin up. Her green eyes are raw, no shield, no bravado. She doesn't blink.

---

### S04 | 9-13s
> CU, two faces, camera from the side

Mika's green eyes locked on Ethan's brown eyes. A tear breaks free from her right eye — it runs down her cheek, catches the light from the hallway, drips off her jaw. She doesn't wipe it. Her lower lip trembles twice. She takes a half step forward. The gap between their faces closes — twelve inches, eight inches, six. Ethan doesn't step back. For the first time in the entire series: he holds his ground. His heel stays planted.

---

### --- TRANSITION 1: Mika 직진 → 감정 정점 → 후퇴 ---

---

### S05 | 13-17s
> Extreme CU, split composition — faces in profile

Three inches apart. Mika's eyes drop to Ethan's mouth. Linger. Climb back to his eyes. Her breath is visible — short, warm puffs that touch his chin. His breath is rapid, shallow. Mika leans forward — one more millimeter — then stops. Everything stops. The air between their lips is electric. Neither moves.

---

### S06 | 17-20s
> Medium, camera from the doorway

Mika's grip on Ethan's wrist releases. Her fingers uncurl one by one. She steps back — one step. Wipes her right cheek hard with the heel of her hand. She turns. Walks out. Fast. Three strides to the door. Doesn't look back. The door stays open — she doesn't close it. Ethan stands there, arm still extended at the angle she held it, mouth open around words that never formed.

---

### --- TRANSITION 2: Mika 퇴장 → Serena+Lily 진심 고백 ---

---

### S07 | 20-25s
> Medium, camera from living room entrance, daylight

Time cut — next day, afternoon. Living room. Warm sunlight through windows. Serena stands in front of Ethan. She's dressed differently — simple white blouse, jeans. Hair pulled back in a ponytail instead of loose and flowing. No heels — flat white sneakers. She looks smaller. Less armored. She holds out a small matte-black box toward Ethan, both hands cupped underneath it.

---

### S08 | 25-29s
> CU, the box opening, camera from above their hands

Ethan takes the box. Opens it. Inside on cotton: a braided leather bracelet — thin, handmade, imperfect. Not expensive. Not Serena's usual polished style. He looks up at her. Serena's face: no smirk, no calculated tilt, no seductive narrowing. Just wide dark eyes, lips pressed together, chin slightly raised in nervous vulnerability. First time Ethan has seen her without armor. His eyes widen.

---

### S09 | 29-33s
> Medium, camera from the opposite side

Lily appears from around the corner. She walks toward Ethan — no bouncing, no running, no "MINE!" energy. She holds out a folded piece of paper with both hands — a letter. Her fingers are slightly shaking. The paper has small wrinkles from being folded and unfolded multiple times (she rewrote it). She holds it out at arm's length, big hazel eyes blinking rapidly.

---

### S10 | 33-37s
> CU, Ethan's face from front

Ethan holding the bracelet box in his right hand. The letter in his left. He looks at the bracelet. At the letter. At Serena on his right. At Lily on his left. His expression isn't panic. Isn't embarrassment. It's something entirely new — overwhelmed realization. His eyebrows lift. His eyes soften and widen at the same time. His chin drops. These aren't games anymore. None of this was ever a game. He swallows hard.

---

### --- TRANSITION 3: 진심 인식 → Tasha 방문 → Diana 폭탄 ---

---

### S11 | 37-42s
> Medium, camera from inside Ethan's room, facing the door, night

Night. Ethan sits on the bed edge. The bracelet is on his right wrist. The letter unfolded beside him on the blanket. His bedroom door opens — no slam this time. Quiet. Tasha. She stands in the doorway. No silk camisole. No seductive nightwear. A plain grey sweater and joggers. Hair unstyled. No performance. Her expression is different from every other moment in the series: serious, searching, terrified.

---

### S12 | 42-46s
> Medium, camera from beside the bed

Tasha takes one step into the room. The floorboard creaks. Ethan stands. They face each other — two feet apart. Tasha opens her mouth. Closes it. Her eyes are wet but she's holding it. Ethan opens his mouth.

**DIALOGUE:** "Tasha..." (Ethan, 1 word, voice breaking)

His lips move to say more — the muscles of his mouth forming syllables — but —

---

### S13 | 46-50s | CLIFFHANGER
> Rapid cuts: phone → each face

From downstairs: a phone rings. Loud, piercing, cutting through the silence. Cut to: living room coffee table, phone screen lit up — "MOM" with Diana's face, incoming video call. Cut to: Ethan and Tasha both look toward the sound. Cut to: phone screen — Diana's smiling face, text bubble appearing: "Coming home tomorrow!"

Rapid cuts between five frozen faces:
- Ethan — mouth still open, eyes wide
- Tasha — hand raised toward Ethan, suspended mid-reach
- Mika — standing at her bedroom door across the hall, red eyes
- Serena — on the living room sofa, bracelet box still in her sight
- Lily — next to Serena, letter still in Ethan's direction

Five statues. Five stopped heartbeats.

CUT TO BLACK.
