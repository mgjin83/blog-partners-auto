```
Medium two-shot of a grey tabby cat-face figure confronting
a calico cat-face figure in a dim flower shop.
Ash's face shows rage mixed with despair. Yellow eyes burning.
Luna looks up with calm exhaustion in green eyes.
Flowers between them on the counter.
Camera slow push-in on both faces.
Thin strips of daylight through blinds creating bars of light across both figures.

Sound: heavy breathing, footsteps approaching.
[Ash, angry desperate male voice]: "I know everything. What you've been doing."
Silence. Luna says nothing.
[Ash, louder shaking voice]: "I never asked you to do that!"
[Luna, quiet calm female voice looking up]: "You did the same thing."
[Ash, voice breaking]: "That's different!"
[Luna, steady quiet]: "How?"
Long devastating silence. Only breathing.
Music: none. Complete silence during dialogue exchange.
```
